# Treatment

## Title

Spark of Lucid Light

## Logline

After a global event called the Spark gives some people fantastic abilities and makes them targets, Jet Black discovers his dream power is real when a government capture robot tears him from his apartment and forces him to choose between hiding and joining the resistance.

## Central Conflict

Jet wants to help the evolved but believes he is powerless in the waking world. When a giant adaptive drone identifies him as evolved, he has to accept that his dream-born Lucid Light is real before the state can turn him into a lab subject.

## Theme

Freedom is not just escape from capture. It is the ability to choose what to do with power once fear has made neutrality impossible.

## Ending

Jet chooses to leave with Bronze Bear and the resistance, accepting fugitive life because it lets him fight for other evolved people to have the choice he almost lost.

## Emotional Arc

Jet begins as a superhero fan who feels guilty and helpless while evolved people are hunted. The robot's attack turns his disbelief into terror, then Bronze Bear's rescue gives him a model for action. By the final page, Jet has not won freedom back; he has chosen a harder path that gives his new power meaning.

## Cast

- Jet Black: protagonist, superhero fan, lucid dreamer, newly revealed evolved target.
- Bronze Bear: resistance rescuer with metallic skin and sharp bear claws.
- Adaptive capture robot: giant government drone built to seize and study evolved people.
- The Resistance: underground group evacuating evolved targets before capture.

## Page Count

Eight pages total: cover plus seven story pages.
