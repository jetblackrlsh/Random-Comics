# Character And Reference Bible

## Jet Black

- Role: protagonist, first-person narrator, superhero fan, lucid dreamer, newly awakened evolved person.
- Age range: adult, late 20s to 30s.
- Identity details from attached reference: Black man with warm brown skin, long black dreadlocks past the shoulders, full black beard and mustache, friendly expressive face, dark eyes, and a relaxed posture that can shift into panic, disbelief, and focused resolve.
- Outfit from attached reference: black hoodie with white zipper trim over a dark graphic T-shirt, black pants, white sneakers, and a black crossbody bag worn at the front of the torso.
- Signature power: Lucid Light, a cosmic purple energy with star motes and violet lensing. In dreams it reshapes reality; in the waking world it first erupts as a defensive shockwave around Jet's body and hands.
- Continuity notes: keep Jet's face, long dreadlocks, full beard, black layered outfit, crossbody bag, and white sneakers consistent on every page. Treat any shirt graphic as incidental clothing detail, not as a title or name. After the robot grabs him, he looks sleep-disoriented, terrified, then intensely focused.

## Bronze Bear

- Role: evolved resistance operative and rescuer.
- Age range: adult, 30s to 40s.
- Visual identifiers: heavily built superhuman with bronze-metal skin, muscular frame, powerful shoulders, sharp metallic bear claws extending from both hands, tactical black suit or harness, and a simple resistance insignia on his chest: a black letter R inside a large red circle.
- Movement: drops from a stealth jet without a parachute, slashes through robot armor, leaps through the air, and absorbs impact like a meteor.
- Expression range: fierce, protective, urgent, and matter-of-fact after landing.
- Continuity notes: keep his metal skin bronze rather than silver, keep the claws visible in action panels, and keep the red-circle resistance mark on his chest when the chest is visible.

## Adaptive Capture Robot

- Role: government-built antagonist and symbol of the anti-evolved crackdown.
- Visual identifiers: giant humanoid drone robot, several stories tall, matte white and gunmetal adaptive armor plates, heavy segmented hands, bright scanning eyes, surveillance sensors, moving hazard lights, and modular limbs that look built for capture rather than rescue.
- Behavior: smashes through an apartment ceiling, grabs Jet without warning, scans him, announces target acquisition through machine text or a rectangular status panel, and adapts while damaged.
- Continuity notes: make the robot huge enough to hold Jet in one hand. Damage to its face from Bronze Bear's claws should persist after the attack.

## Resistance Stealth Jet

- Role: escape vehicle and resistance symbol.
- Visual identifiers: matte black stealth aircraft, angular body, no national markings, red resistance-circle insignia near the side hatch, hidden in a swamp clearing after the rescue.
- Continuity notes: first appears overhead at night, then appears parked low among reeds and cypress trees near Jet's apartment.

## Jet's Apartment And Nearby Swamp

- Apartment: modest apartment bedroom at night, broken ceiling, dust, insulation, city lights, and emergency glow from the robot outside.
- Swamp: dark wetland near the apartment building, cypress trees, reeds, black water reflecting purple Lucid Light and red aircraft lights.
