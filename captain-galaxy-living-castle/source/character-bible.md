# Character Bible

## Global Photo-Comic Look

Every page should look like a full realistic candid photo-comic page assembled from documentary, handheld, phone-photo, or photojournalistic images. The environments are cosmic and surreal, but the camera feel remains physical: natural imperfect framing, believable practical light, realistic lens flare, dust, reflections, motion blur, and photoreal VFX integrated into the scene. Use clean rectangular caption boxes only, with all readable text generated directly inside the page art.

## Captain Galaxy

- Role: Current champion of the Storyverse and host of the Cosmic Castle.
- Age and build: Adult man in his early forties, tall, athletic, square jaw, tired heroic face, medium-brown skin, close-cropped black hair visible when the helmet angle allows it.
- Outfit: Deep cobalt-blue armored hero suit with silver star-map seams, gold star emblem on the chest, silver gauntlets, silver boots, and a short weathered red cape.
- Signature prop: Radiant circular star shield, metallic silver rim, dark blue face, gold starburst center, glowing white-gold constellations across the surface. The shield can be dropped, raised, and expanded to impossible size, but it should remain recognizable.
- Expression range: Proud disbelief, defensive anger, horror, begging fear, trembling resolve, sacrificial determination.
- Continuity: Keep the same face, suit, cape, and star shield across all pages. Damage, dust, sweat, and fear can accumulate after the biological reveal.

## The Cosmic Castle

- Role: Living fortress-ship, manipulator, reluctant truth-teller, and doomed companion.
- Exterior: Monumental castle-shaped spacecraft made of pearl-white towers, stained-glass star windows, silver buttresses, and glowing engines, drifting through a field of bubble universes.
- Control room disguise: Polished white-gold command hall with arched walls, glowing star maps, crystal consoles, and elegant castle architecture.
- Hidden biology: Walls shift open to reveal red organic tissue, translucent nerve cords, blue-white veins, bone-like ribs, wet membrane, and a huge beating heart behind the back wall.
- Personality shown visually: The castle has no face. Its speech should be implied through caption boxes and the environment reacting around Captain Galaxy.
- Continuity: The castle should feel both architectural and alive, never a normal spaceship and never a monster with a humanoid face.

## Past Captain Galaxies

- Role: Former hosts discarded by the Cosmic Castle.
- Visible identifiers: Skeletons wearing older, faded versions of Captain Galaxy's blue suit, tarnished star emblems, ripped red capes, and cracked dusty star shields.
- Mood: Tragic evidence, not action enemies. They lie around the heart in cobwebbed alcoves and collapsed heroic poses.

## The Storyverse

- Role: The totality of stories that Captain Galaxy protects.
- Visual motif: Countless transparent bubble universes suspended in deep space, each containing miniature worlds, comic cities, fantasy castles, alien skies, oceans, suns, and narrative symbols.
- The flood: An unimaginably vast wall of cosmic sea water pouring over the bubble universes, lit by stars and crackling with impossible story-light.
