# Page Script

Global style for every page: full realistic candid photo-comic page, 4:5 portrait aspect ratio, complete page with multiple panels or a splash panel, photoreal documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism look, natural imperfect framing, believable real-world lighting, photoreal cosmic VFX, clean rectangular caption boxes only. No speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating dialogue, no subtitles, no watermark, no logo, no blank text boxes. All readable story text must be generated directly inside the page art. This edition has 8 pages: cover plus 7 story pages.

## Page 01 - Cover

Story role: Signal a cosmic superhero tragedy about a living castle and a universe-saving sacrifice.

Layout: Dramatic realistic photo-comic cover splash. Captain Galaxy stands in the cracked control room of the Cosmic Castle, holding the radiant star shield high. Behind him, white-gold castle walls split open into red living tissue and glowing nerves. Far behind the shattered wall, bubble universes face an enormous wave of cosmic sea water.

Caption boxes/text inside art: Title text: "CAPTAIN GALAXY: THE LIVING CASTLE". Small caption box: "Every victory had a heartbeat."

Panel 4: Captain Galaxy stares at the walls, defensive and angry. Panel 5: the arched wall panels glow like a throat preparing to speak.

Caption boxes inside art: "Captain Galaxy had trusted these walls through every impossible victory." "Then the Cosmic Castle spoke." "It said the victories had never been his alone."
