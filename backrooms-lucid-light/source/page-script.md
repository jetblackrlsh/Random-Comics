# Page Script

## Page 1: Cover

Story role: Signal a lucid dreamer trapped in the Backrooms without showing the escape.

Layout: One dominant vertical cover image with three small glitch-strip inset panels along the right edge.

Caption/title text to generate in the page art: Title text: "BACKROOMS LUCID LIGHT". Small caption box: "Jet Black knew every dream had a door. This place did not."

Visual beats: Jet Black stands in a yellow Backrooms hallway, damp and tense, one hand raised with only a tiny failed violet spark between his fingers. Behind him, the supply crate sits innocently at the far corner, its shadow hinting at jagged teeth. Insets show a black leather couch, a hand phasing through cushion, and a dark static void.

## Page 2: Before The Stream

Story role: Give readers an everything-you-need-to-know primer before the story begins.

Layout: Clean in-universe dossier page with four large labeled panels and a small central portrait of Jet surrounded by faint purple Lucid Light.

Caption/title text to generate in the page art: Header text: "EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW". Label text: "JET BLACK", "THE STORYVERSE", "LUCID LIGHT", "LUCID CONDUITS".

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Jet Black: "Lucid dreamer. Daily streamer. Storyverse explorer."
- The Storyverse: "A web of bubble universes containing every possible story."
- Lucid Light: "Purple cosmic energy that lets Jet reshape dreams."
- Lucid Conduits: "Objects or people that hold lucidity when Jet's power is hidden."

Visual beats: Jet appears in the center as a grounded portrait matching the reference, with small surrounding vignettes: glowing bubble universes floating in darkness, purple Lucid Light swirling around a hand, and ordinary objects glowing as Lucid Conduits. The Backrooms should not dominate this page; it should feel like a clear primer before the horror starts.

## Page 3: Before The Stream

Story role: Establish Jet's ordinary afternoon, his confidence, and the no-clipping incident.

Layout: Four rectangular panels in a readable grid.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "Thursday, just before my two o'clock stream."
- Small caption: "Lucid Light had made me fearless in dreams."
- Lower caption: "Then my hand went through the couch."

Visual beats: Jet rises from a black leather couch in his apartment. His hand sinks into the cushion. His foot phases through white carpet. He drops through the floor while the green stove clock glows nearby.

## Page 4: Static Below

Story role: Move Jet from denial into the Backrooms.

Layout: Five panels, with the middle panel a vertical fall through static.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "Reality opened underneath me."
- Middle caption: "Color. Static. Glitches."
- Final caption: "Wet carpet found me first."

Visual beats: Jet tumbles through a black void filled with colorful static and broken glitch rectangles. He blacks out. His hand presses into soaked beige carpet in darkness. Fluorescent lights snap on overhead, revealing yellow wallpaper and endless empty halls.

## Page 5: No Light

Story role: Show that Jet recognizes the Backrooms and cannot summon Lucid Light.

Layout: Four panels with tight handheld angles.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "I knew this place."
- Small caption: "The Backrooms."
- Lower caption: "I pictured Lucid Light. Nothing answered."

Visual beats: Jet stands in the fluorescent maze, soaked and alarmed. He aims his fingers toward a blank wall as if creating a portal. Purple cosmic energy flickers only as faint sparks in his eyes and fingertips, then dies. A long hallway waits with no exit.

## Page 6: Looking For A Conduit

Story role: Turn Jet's search into obsessive wandering and introduce the possibility of supplies.

Layout: Six small panels with repeating corridors to create disorientation.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "In dreams, lucidity can hide inside objects."
- Middle caption: "I call them Lucid Conduits."
- Lower caption: "Somewhere in the maze, I hoped one was waiting."

Visual beats: Jet moves through repeated yellow halls, checking corners, touching wallpaper, watching fluorescent lights hum. The panels become nearly identical except for Jet's growing exhaustion. In the last panel, a wooden crate rests alone under a ceiling light.

## Page 7: The Crate Opens

Story role: Reveal the false hope as a monster and begin the chase.

Layout: Three panels: approach, transformation, immediate pursuit.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "Supplies existed here. Maybe hope did too."
- Middle caption: "The crate ran at me."
- Lower caption: "Then it laughed."

Visual beats: Jet hurries toward the scuffed crate. Wooden legs burst from its underside. A jagged toothy mouth splits across the front boards. Jet spins away as the crate charges, wet carpet kicking up behind both of them.

## Page 8: Use The Rules

Story role: Force Jet to solve the problem without powers.

Layout: Five fast panels with motion blur and sharp corner turns.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "Every corner brought it closer."
- Middle caption: "No light. No dream items. No time."
- Lower caption: "No-clipping got me in."
- Final caption: "Maybe it could get me out."

Visual beats: Jet sprints through repeating halls while the crate monster stays just behind him. He glances at walls and corners, remembering game logic. His expression shifts from panic to desperate calculation. In the final panel, he lowers his shoulder and runs directly toward a tight yellow corner.

## Page 9: Back Through The Couch

Story role: Resolve the chase with Jet no-clipping out and choosing recovery over performance.

Layout: Four panels ending with a quiet apartment panel.

Caption boxes to generate in the page art:
- Top caption: "I jumped where the world was weakest."
- Middle caption: "For one second, I became impossible."
- Lower caption: "The clock had not moved."
- Final caption: "The livestream could wait. I needed a shower."

Visual beats: Jet collides with the Backrooms corner and phases through yellow wallpaper as the crate snaps behind him. He rockets upward through a black glitch void. He bursts out of the black leather couch in his apartment, soaked, sweaty, and gasping. Final panel: Jet stands near the kitchen, dripping onto the white carpet, looking at the green stove clock reading just before 2 PM.
