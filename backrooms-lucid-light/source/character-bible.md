# Character And Reference Bible

## Jet Black, lucid dreamer and streamer

- Role: protagonist and first-person narrator.
- Age range: adult, late 20s to 30s.
- Identity details from reference: Black man with warm brown skin, long black dreadlocks past the shoulders, full black beard and mustache, friendly expressive face, dark eyes, and relaxed posture that shifts into panic and focus.
- Outfit from reference: black hoodie with white zipper trim over a dark graphic T-shirt, black pants, white sneakers, and a black crossbody bag worn at the front of the torso.
- Signature motif: purple cosmic Lucid Light, normally imagined as bright violet starfield energy around his hands, but in this story it only appears as failed flickers in his mind or tiny sparks that will not project.
- Expression range: comfortable anticipation at home, shock while falling, frightened denial in the Backrooms, desperate concentration during the chase, exhausted relief after escape.
- Continuity notes: keep Jet's hair, beard, black layered outfit, crossbody bag, and white sneakers consistent on every page. By the Backrooms pages, he becomes damp with carpet water and increasingly sweaty; by the final page he is wet and smells of mold.

## The Backrooms

- Role: main setting and hostile liminal maze.
- Visual identifiers from reference: yellowed patterned wallpaper, beige damp carpet, repeating rectangular corridors, square support columns, low drop ceiling, long fluorescent light panels, weak green-yellow tint, stale office-space architecture, and empty corners that imply more rooms beyond the frame.
- Sensory details: wet carpet under Jet's hands, mold smell, constant fluorescent hum, sudden harsh illumination, and a sense that every turn repeats.
- Continuity notes: keep the Backrooms realistic, bland, and oppressive. Avoid fantasy architecture; the horror comes from ordinary office materials stretched too far.

## Mimic supply crate

- Role: obstacle and antagonist.
- Appearance: at first a scuffed wooden supply crate on the wet carpet, then a creature with splintered running legs, a hinged mouth, jagged wooden teeth, and warped boards flexing like muscle.
- Behavior: it pretends to be useful, then sprints after Jet while cackling.
- Continuity notes: keep the crate small enough to plausibly look like a supply box at rest, but fast and predatory once it moves.

## Jet's apartment

- Role: reality anchor and escape point.
- Visual identifiers: black leather couch, white carpet floor, kitchen visible nearby, digital stove clock with green numbers showing just before 2 PM.
- Continuity notes: the apartment should feel normal and lived-in, contrasting sharply with the damp yellow maze.

## Visual Rules

- Full realistic candid photo-comic pages, documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism look.
- Caption boxes only; no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating dialogue.
- All readable story text must be generated directly inside the page art.
- Keep Jet visually consistent with the provided protagonist reference and keep the Backrooms consistent with the attached setting reference.
