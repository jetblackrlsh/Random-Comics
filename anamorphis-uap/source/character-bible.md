# Character Bible

## Eli Voss

- Role: protagonist, independent comic-book creator.
- Age: late 20s.
- Build and posture: lean, slightly hunched from long drawing sessions, tense shoulders that loosen as the story progresses.
- Face and hair: oval face, tired brown eyes, short messy dark-brown hair, light stubble.
- Skin tone: light-medium warm skin.
- Outfit: charcoal hoodie, faded black jeans, worn gray sneakers. At home he often wears thin rectangular glasses. In the final pages the hoodie is still charcoal but catches warmer city light.
- Signature props: battered sketchbook, black ink pen, laptop with video-editing timeline, messy desk with comic thumbnails and reference printouts.
- Expression range: exhausted, defensive, quietly awed, then focused and more alive.

## Mara Keene

- Role: editor, friend, and voice of practical skepticism.
- Age: early 30s.
- Build and posture: average build, upright posture, controlled gestures.
- Face and hair: heart-shaped face, sharp attentive eyes, dark coily hair pulled into a high puff.
- Skin tone: deep brown skin.
- Outfit: olive utility jacket over a cream sweater, dark trousers, small silver hoop earrings.
- Signature props: tablet, coffee cup, messenger bag.
- Expression range: concerned, analytical, apologetic, quietly moved by Eli's final comic.

## The Anamorphis UAP

- Role: alien mystery and symbol of wonder.
- Stable visible identifiers: luminous faceless humanoid silhouette made of pearly white, cyan, and pale gold light; no eyes, mouth, nose, hair, clothing, armor, or mechanical parts; body edges blur and reshape as if made of living light; long arms and human-like torso; lower body sometimes trails into radiating vapor.
- Motion: flying silently, impossibly fast, leaving faint light smears and multiple ghost positions across frames.
- Behavior: watches from above; never attacks, descends, helps, or speaks.
- Important distinction: it must not look like a drone swarm, aircraft, robot, angel with wings, superhero, or person in a glowing suit. It should look like a morphing light being because it lacks normal surface detail.

## Recurring Locations

- Eli's apartment studio: small city apartment, crowded desk, monitor glow, stacks of sketches, muted gray morning light, practical clutter.
- Online-video workspace: laptop screen with grainy military-style aerial footage and waveform/timeline UI, no real agency names or logos.
- Street and coffee shop: ordinary city environment where skeptical explanations flatten the mood.
- Rooftop at dusk: low city skyline, gray clouds opening to warmer light, sky large enough for a silent luminous figure.
- Comic-release scene: printed or tablet comic pages in readers' hands, city dusk behind them.
