# Page Script

Every page is a full realistic candid photo-comic page in 4:5 portrait aspect ratio. All readable story text must be generated directly inside rectangular caption boxes in the page art. Use caption boxes only; no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating subtitles, no blank text boxes, no later text overlay.

## Page 1 - Cover

Use case: full realistic candid photo-comic page
Asset type: cover, 4:5 portrait aspect ratio
Primary request: Create a complete realistic photo-comic cover page, not a single poster, not anime, not illustration.
Story role: Introduce Eli, the gray world, and the impossible luminous watcher above him.
Character/reference bible for continuity: Eli Voss is a late-20s lean comic-book creator with an oval face, tired brown eyes, short messy dark-brown hair, light stubble, light-medium warm skin, charcoal hoodie, faded black jeans, worn gray sneakers, thin rectangular glasses, battered sketchbook, black ink pen. The Anamorphis UAP is a luminous faceless humanoid silhouette made of pearly white, cyan, and pale gold light, no eyes, mouth, nose, hair, clothing, armor, mechanical parts, or wings, body edges blurring and reshaping like living light, long arms, human-like torso, lower body trailing into radiating vapor.
Page layout: Dramatic candid-photo cover composition. Eli stands on a city rooftop with sketchbook open, tiny against a gray skyline. Above him, the Anamorphis UAP hangs high in the sky, faceless and watchful, slightly motion-blurred as if captured from imperfect phone footage. The skyline below feels drained of color except for the being's light reflecting on Eli's face.
Caption boxes: Small title box generated in the art: "ANAMORPHIS UAP". Lower caption box: "When the world went gray, wonder looked down."
Visual beats: Eli looks up in exhausted awe; pages of sketches flutter; the light being does not descend.
Setting and props: Rooftop, city skyline, sketchbook, pen, low clouds, faint screenlike video grain in the sky.
Photo style: realistic candid photo-comic, documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism look, natural imperfect framing, believable dusk lighting, photoreal luminous VFX, clean rectangular caption boxes.
Constraints: 4:5 portrait full page, caption boxes only, no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating dialogue, no subtitles, no watermark, no logo, no extra unreadable text. All readable story text must be generated directly inside the page art.
