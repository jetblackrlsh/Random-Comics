# Page Script

Global style for every page: full realistic candid photo-comic page, 4:5 portrait aspect ratio, complete page with multiple panels or a splash panel, photoreal documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism look, natural imperfect framing, believable real-world lighting, photoreal imaginary paint VFX, toy-scale macro photography, clean rectangular caption boxes only. No speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating dialogue, no subtitles, no watermark, no logo, no blank text boxes. All readable story text must be generated directly inside the page art. This edition has 8 pages: cover plus 7 story pages.

## Page 01 - Cover

Story role: Signal the adult writer, action-figure fantasy, office-turned-city, and Dust Demon threat.

Layout: Dramatic realistic photo-comic cover splash. Foreground shows the six-inch Action Figure Hero in blue-and-silver armor with a red plastic scarf standing on Toby's cluttered office desk. Behind him, imaginary paint transforms a laptop into a glowing supercomputer and storage bins into city buildings. The dusty window blinds spiral into a portal where the giant gray Dust Demon looms. Toby's human hand and tired face appear huge and fading in the background, as if the daydream is taking over.

Caption boxes/text inside art: Title text: "FIGURE FIDDLER: DUST DEMON". Small caption box: "Writer's block met its match in a toy box."

## Page 02 - The Old Habit

Story role: Establish Toby's writer's block, his lifelong attachment to figures, and the action figure that starts the daydream.

Layout: Five panels. Panel 1: Toby sits at a cluttered office desk, laptop open to a blank document, hands in his hair. Panel 2: shelves and bins crowded with action figures, some dusty, taking up space. Panel 3: close-up of Toby picking up the blue-and-silver Action Figure Hero with red scarf. Panel 4: Toby fiddles with the figure's arms and shoulders, expression softening. Panel 5: a blue-white electric spark begins traveling from Toby's head down his arm into the figure.

Caption boxes inside art: "Toby had been told to grow up." "He kept the figures anyway." "When words failed, his hands remembered how to play."

## Page 03 - Into The Figure

Story role: Shift consciousness from Toby into the figure and transform the office into the toy-scale city.

Layout: Six panels. Panel 1: blue-white electricity streams through Toby's hand into the figure's chest. Panel 2: point-of-view from the tiny figure looking up at Toby as a gentle giant holding him. Panel 3: Toby's face fades into soft blur while imaginary paint splashes across the desk. Panel 4: the desk becomes a headquarters platform with toy-scale lights and walls. Panel 5: storage bins reshape into towering city buildings around the desktop. Panel 6: the Action Figure Hero stands alone at toy scale, ready in the transformed office city.

Caption boxes inside art: "His mind became a spark." "The giant faded." "The office accepted a new coat of wonder."

## Page 04 - Trouble At The Window

Story role: Introduce the supercomputer alert, portal, and Dust Demon.

Layout: Six panels. Panel 1: the Action Figure Hero approaches Toby's laptop, now covered in imaginary paint and glowing as a supercomputer. Panel 2: close-up of a trouble alert on the transformed laptop screen showing a portal at the edge of the city. Panel 3: the hero turns toward the dusty office window. Panel 4: imaginary paint splashes over the blinds and glass. Panel 5: the window spirals into an interdimensional portal. Panel 6: dust from the blinds rises into the giant humanoid Dust Demon with hollow amber eyes.

Caption boxes inside art: "The supercomputer found trouble." "Dust had learned a shape." "At the edge of the city, a demon arrived."

## Page 05 - Punching Smoke

Story role: Show the hero's brave but ineffective attack against an intangible enemy.

Layout: Five action panels. Panel 1: the Action Figure Hero leaps from desk headquarters toward the Dust Demon, red scarf snapping. Panel 2: his plastic fist punches through the demon's dusty chest without impact. Panel 3: another kick passes harmlessly through swirling gray dust. Panel 4: the Dust Demon swats him down with a huge smoky hand. Panel 5: the hero lands hard near bins transformed into buildings as dust begins raining over the city.

Caption boxes inside art: "He charged like every hero should." "But courage could not bruise dust." "The demon answered with a storm."

## Page 06 - The Corrupted Collection

Story role: Escalate the threat as dust corrupts the action figures living inside the transformed buildings and corners the hero.

Layout: Six panels. Panel 1: gray dust settles over action figures in bin-buildings, their eyes glowing amber. Panel 2: corrupted figures step into the streets, stiff and possessed. Panel 3: the Action Figure Hero raises both hands, trying to reason with them. Panel 4: the crowd keeps advancing, too far gone under dust. Panel 5: the Dust Demon towers in the window portal behind them. Panel 6: the hero backs into the real handheld vacuum at the edge of the desk-city, realization dawning.

Caption boxes inside art: "Dust reached the ones he loved." "He begged them to fight it." "Then his back touched an answer."

## Page 07 - Holy Hoverbike

Story role: Transform the handheld vacuum into the Holy Hoverbike and free the corrupted figures.

Layout: Six panels. Panel 1: imaginary paint splashes over the handheld vacuum. Panel 2: fantasy view of the hero assembling a hoverbike from chrome scrap in a narrow alley between bin-buildings. Panel 3: the handheld vacuum becomes the Holy Hoverbike, still echoing its nozzle and handle, with blue-white holy engines. Panel 4: the hero launches over the mob of corrupted figures. Panel 5: dust streams out of the figures and into the bike's glowing intake. Panel 6: cleansed action figures look up with bright normal colors as the bike races toward the portal.

Caption boxes inside art: "Imagination made a machine." "Faith lit the engines." "One clean pass gave the city back its souls."

## Page 08 - Back To The Keyboard

Story role: Resolve the demon battle and reveal Toby cleaned his office while playing, ending with renewed inspiration.

Layout: Six panels. Panel 1: the Dust Demon stomps toward the Holy Hoverbike, foot made of swirling blinds-dust. Panel 2: the hero performs a sharp stunt turn beneath the stomp. Panel 3: the hoverbike's vacuum intake pulls the demon into a spiraling stream of dust. Panel 4: sudden black transition panel with tiny blue-white spark. Panel 5: Toby opens his eyes in the real office, holding the Action Figure Hero in one hand and the handheld vacuum in the other; the desk, bins, and blinds are visibly cleaner. Panel 6: Toby sets the hero beside the laptop and types with inspired focus.

Caption boxes inside art: "The demon tried to crush belief." "The bike swallowed the storm." "Toby woke to a clean room and a waiting story."
