# Character Bible

## Global Photo-Comic Look

Every page should look like a full realistic candid photo-comic page assembled from documentary, handheld, phone-photo, or photojournalistic images. The real-world office should feel like a believable writer's room with shelves, bins, loose figures, a laptop, dust on blinds, and a handheld vacuum. The fantasy overlay should look like photoreal imaginary paint splashing across real objects, transforming them into toy-scale headquarters, buildings, portals, and super tech. Use clean rectangular caption boxes only, with all readable text generated directly inside the page art.

## Toby

- Role: Adult writer and action-figure collector.
- Age and build: Man in his mid-30s to early 40s, average build, tired but gentle face, light stubble, thoughtful posture.
- Hair and eyes: Short messy dark brown hair, brown eyes, expressive eyebrows.
- Outfit: Soft gray hoodie, dark T-shirt, jeans, casual house socks.
- Signature props: Laptop on a crowded desk, six-inch action figure in hand, compact handheld vacuum.
- Expression range: Frustrated writer's block, embarrassed affection for toys, focused daydreaming, warm relief when inspiration returns.

## The Action Figure Hero

- Role: Toby's daydream avatar and the hero of the toy city.
- Scale and material: Six-inch articulated plastic action figure, photographed at toy scale, with visible joints, molded seams, small scuffs, and realistic plastic shine.
- Design: Blue-and-silver sci-fi paladin armor, red plastic scarf, painted green eyes, squared heroic jaw, tiny utility belt, silver boots, no helmet.
- Signature visual motif: Small heroic silhouette against giant office objects, blue-white spark of Toby's consciousness, imaginary paint converting ordinary office clutter into epic terrain.
- Continuity: Always keep the same blue-and-silver armor, red scarf, painted green eyes, plastic articulation joints, and toy scale.

## The Dust Demon

- Role: Antagonist formed from neglected dust on the office blinds.
- Appearance: Giant humanoid made of swirling gray dust, long arms, clawed smoky fingers, hollow amber eyes, particulate body with ragged edges.
- Behavior: Intangible against punches, able to infect dusty action figures, grows from the window blinds and portal.
- Continuity: The demon should always read as dust from a real office window transformed by imagination, not as a solid monster.

## Corrupted Figures

- Role: Other action figures in the imagined city, temporarily controlled by dust.
- Appearance: Mixed toy citizens and heroes from Toby's collection, different costumes and sizes, all with a gray dust coating and faint amber glow in their eyes.
- Behavior: Stiff toy-like movement, surrounding the hero in a crowd, then returning to clean colorful plastic after the hoverbike passes overhead.

## Holy Hoverbike

- Role: The transformed handheld vacuum and decisive rescue vehicle.
- Real-world base: Compact black-and-white handheld vacuum with nozzle, dust chamber, vents, and handle.
- Fantasy form: Holy Hoverbike made from discarded super tech, chrome scrap, glowing blue-white engines, tiny vacuum intake beneath the frame, faint gold light suggesting faith-powered energy.
- Continuity: It should still visually echo the handheld vacuum, especially the nozzle, intake, and handle.

## Toby's Office

- Real-world setting: Small home office with a cluttered desk, laptop, bins of action figures, shelves, dusty window blinds, loose notes, charging cables, and the handheld vacuum nearby.
- Fantasy transformation: Desk becomes headquarters, bins become city buildings, laptop becomes a supercomputer, window becomes an interdimensional portal, dust on blinds becomes the Dust Demon.
