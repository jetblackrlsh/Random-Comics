# Character Bible

## Bun-Bun

- Role: Young Valordian explorer and reluctant rescuer.
- Age: Early 20s adult.
- Face and hair: Round face, alert dark eyes, black hair always styled into two high space buns with loose strands at the temples.
- Skin and blood: White leather-like translucent skin; glowing cyan-magenta neon veins and arteries visible beneath the skin, especially at wrists, throat, cheekbones, and hands. Her glow weakens in darkness and under volcanic ash.
- Outfit: Sleek pearlescent white exploration suit with charcoal seams, compact utility belt, scuffed white boots, no helmet inside atmosphere. Suit becomes ash-stained and torn after the crash and cave fight.
- Body language: Curious, quick, scholarly, then increasingly desperate; she shields the ore close to her chest after escaping.
- Powers: Flight through a repulsive anti-gravity field that looks like a faint transparent distortion halo; super strength and durability, both weakened when deprived of light.

## Valordian Crew

- Role: Stranded scholars and engineers.
- Appearance: Adults with black hair, white translucent leather-like skin, and visible glowing neon circulatory systems. Their veins glow dimmer under the ash.
- Outfit: Matching white and graphite exploration uniforms; some wear tool harnesses, medical bands, or flight harnesses.
- Signature props: Damaged silver exploration ship, broken faster-than-light messaging core, handheld diagnostic tools, emergency lamps.

## Communication Officer

- Role: Crew member who discovers the messaging system is broken.
- Appearance: Middle-aged Valordian with cropped black hair, rectangular face, dim green-blue veins, ash on cheeks.
- Outfit: White uniform with dark collar and cracked wrist console.
- Prop: FTL transmitter console with a fractured crystal socket that needs jungle-world ore.

## Crew Doctor

- Role: Tends Bun-Bun's injuries and witnesses her remorse.
- Appearance: Older Valordian with long black hair tied back, calm narrow face, warm blue glowing veins.
- Outfit: White medical coat over exploration suit, portable medkit, soft amber exam lamp.

## Humanoid Dinosaur Priest

- Role: Guardian of the sacred ore deposit, antagonist through cultural conflict rather than simple villainy.
- Appearance: Towering reptilian humanoid with dark green-scaled skin, heavy brow, amber eyes, clawed hands, long tail, toothy snout, and ceremonial bone-and-vine jewelry.
- Outfit: Weathered volcanic-stone chest plates, woven vine cords, skull charms, ash-painted ritual markings.
- Body language: Reverent near the ore, furious when it is attacked, physically overwhelming in close quarters.
- Signature setting: Bone-strewn cave shrine beneath a luminous ore deposit worshipped as a god.

## Jungle World

- Surface: Dense wet alien jungle, giant fern-like plants, volcanic ash falling like gray snow, orange lava glow in the distance, a clearing scarred by the crashed ship.
- Cave: Black basalt tunnel, wet stone, scattered bones, ritual carvings, smoky torchlight, central ore deposit glowing gold-green.
- Lighting rule: The Valordians' neon circulatory glow is brightest in daylight and dimmest in the ash-black cave.
