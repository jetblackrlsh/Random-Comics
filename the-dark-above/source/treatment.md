# The Dark Above

## Logline

After a late superhero movie, a wealthy family's shortcut through a neglected alley becomes an origin story for something predatory when their young son discovers flight, fangs, and a hunger that spreads.

## Central Conflict

The parents want to understand and save their son, but the alley transforms their parental instincts into vulnerability. The boy's new power is not a gift he controls; it is an infection or possession that uses wonder to get close.

## Theme

Childlike fantasy can become terror when power arrives without conscience, and love becomes dangerous when it refuses to recognize the threat in time.

## Emotional Arc

The story begins with post-movie excitement and family warmth, shifts into unease as the lamp fails, becomes disbelief when the boy is revealed floating above them, and ends in panic as the mother realizes the family she entered the alley with is gone.

## Ending

The final image resolves the story visually: the mother's dropped phone shines upward from the alley floor, revealing the boy and his transformed father flying after her with glowing red eyes and fangs. She escapes the alley for the moment, but the threat has already multiplied.
