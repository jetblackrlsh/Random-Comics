# Character And Reference Bible

## Ethan Vale, the son

- Age: 8 years old.
- Build: small, slim, energetic.
- Face: round face, wide brown eyes, small nose, expressive eyebrows.
- Hair: neat dark brown hair, slightly mussed after running.
- Skin tone: light warm skin.
- Outfit: expensive navy hooded jacket over a pale gray movie T-shirt, dark jeans, clean white sneakers.
- Motif: arms spread like airplane wings when pretending to fly; later floating unnaturally above the alley with glowing red eyes and small sharp fangs.
- Expression range: excited and playful at first, stunned and apologetic during the reveal, then blank and predatory during the attack.

## Dr. Adrian Vale, the father

- Age: early 40s.
- Build: tall, trim, polished.
- Face: angular jaw, controlled expression, short dark hair with faint gray at the temples, brown eyes.
- Skin tone: light warm skin.
- Outfit: tailored charcoal overcoat, white dress shirt, dark trousers, expensive leather shoes.
- Signature prop: black smartphone used as a flashlight.
- Profession cue: calm doctorly composure, practical and analytical when frightened.
- Transformation: after Ethan attacks him, he appears airborne with glowing red eyes and visible fangs, still wearing the charcoal overcoat.

## Camille Vale, the mother

- Age: late 30s.
- Build: slender, elegant.
- Face: oval face, high cheekbones, worried green eyes.
- Hair: shoulder-length auburn hair, carefully styled but loosened by panic.
- Skin tone: light skin with subtle freckles.
- Outfit: camel wool coat, black dress, low heels, pearl earrings.
- Expression range: affectionate amusement, alarmed concern, terror, hyperventilating shock.
- Final state: fleeing out of the alley, backlit by street spill, abandoning the phone behind her.

## Location And Props

- Late-night alley behind a shabby movie theater in a rough part of town.
- Wet pavement, brick walls, dumpsters, fire escape ladders, torn posters, steam from a street grate.
- One overhead sodium streetlamp that flickers amber and then goes black.
- Phone flashlight becomes the main light source after the lamp dies.
- Horror reveal should keep the alley mostly dark, with harsh upward flashlight light and deep shadows.

## Visual Rules

- Full realistic candid photo-comic pages, documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism look.
- Caption boxes only; no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating dialogue.
- All readable story text must be generated directly inside the page art.
- Keep the three Vales visually consistent across every page.
