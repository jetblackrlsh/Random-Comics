A young boy and his wealthy parents walk together through a late-night alley after an exciting superhero movie in a rough part of town. The boy runs ahead pretending to fly while his parents warn him not to go too far. A flickering alley lamp dies, the boy vanishes into the darkness, and the parents hear his apology from above. The father finds him flying overhead with the phone flashlight. The boy describes a prick in his neck and a rush of power. When the father asks to inspect the boy's neck, the boy asks to see his father's neck too, then attacks. The mother finds the dropped phone, cannot bring herself to look upward, and flees. The phone lies on the ground shining up as the flying son and father, now with glowing red eyes and fangs, chase after her.
