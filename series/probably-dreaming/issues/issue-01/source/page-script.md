# Page Script

## Global Prompt Requirements

Every page must be a complete full realistic candid photo-comic page in 4:5 portrait aspect ratio. Use caption boxes only; no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating dialogue, no subtitles, no watermark, no logo. All readable story text, title text, caption text, and any clothing text must be generated directly inside the page art. Do not leave blank caption boxes for later typesetting.

Jet Black continuity: Black man in his 30s with medium-brown skin, long black dreadlocks past his shoulders, full beard and mustache, black zip hoodie with white drawstrings, black shirt under the hoodie with visible colorful text reading "Dream Anime", black pants, white sneakers, small black crossbody/fanny pack across the front. Use realistic candid photo-comic styling based on the provided protagonist reference.

## Page 1: Cover

- Filename: `assets/comic-pages/page-01-cover.png`
- Story role: Distinct cover page for Issue #1, separate from the recurring prologue.
- Layout: One dramatic realistic photo-comic cover image. Jet stands inside his apartment doorway smiling calmly while a zombie leans in from the hallway. Purple Lucid Light faintly glows in the background like a dream clue, but it should not dominate the scene. The severed zombie head can be hinted as a small ominous foreground detail if composition allows.
- Caption boxes:
  - Title text: "Probably Dreaming"
  - Subtitle text: "Issue #1: Bite Me at 4:30"
  - Small cover line: "A zombie knocks. Jet opens."
- Visual beats: The cover should signal absurdist zombie comedy, Jet's calm dream logic, and the ordinary apartment setting invaded by the impossible.

## Page 2: Prologue

- Filename: `assets/comic-pages/page-02.png`
- Story role: Recurring prologue page that explains the series formula while introducing Issue #1.
- Layout: A word-heavier comic prologue page with a large title block and four documentary-style panels: Jet at his apartment desk making AI comics, Jet surrounded by purple Lucid Light in a dreamlike version of the apartment, a close view of an ordinary object acting as a purple-glowing Lucid Conduit, and Jet casually waking up while strange evidence remains nearby.
- Caption boxes:
  - Title text: "Probably Dreaming"
  - Caption 1: "Jet Black treats weird days like lucid dreams."
  - Caption 2: "When Lucid Light works, he bends the dream."
  - Caption 3: "When it does not, he searches for a Lucid Conduit."
  - Caption 4: "Normal always returns. The evidence usually stays."
- Visual beats: Make the page more text-forward than later pages, but still readable and visual. Show Jet's routine, cosmic purple energy, and the core ambiguity.

## Page 4: Story Page Two

- Filename: `assets/comic-pages/page-04.png`
- Story role: Jet intentionally becomes a zombie and removes the zombie's head.
- Layout: Four panels. Panel 1: Jet holds out his arm for the bite. Panel 2: zombie teeth clamp into his arm. Panel 3: Jet puts the zombie in a headlock and cleanly removes the head in stylized horror-comedy framing without excessive gore. Panel 4: Jet holds the severed head theatrically while his skin turns green, hair shifts blue, and eyes glow yellow.
- Caption boxes:

  - Caption 2: "One careful headlock later..."
  - Jet caption: "Alas, poor zombie."
- Visual beats: Keep horror comedic, not splatter-focused. Jet is transforming but still recognizable.

## Page 6: Story Page Four
