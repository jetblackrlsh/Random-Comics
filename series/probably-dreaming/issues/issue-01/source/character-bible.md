## Jet Black

Black man in his 30s with medium-brown skin, long black dreadlocks past his shoulders, full beard and mustache, warm smile, slim-to-average build, relaxed posture, black zip hoodie with white drawstrings, black shirt under the hoodie with visible colorful text reading "Dream Anime", black pants, white sneakers, small black crossbody/fanny pack worn across the front. He is calm, quirky, and disciplined, with body language that treats bizarre events like solvable routine problems.

When zombified, Jet keeps the same hairstyle, beard, hoodie, shirt, pants, sneakers, and crossbody pack, but his skin turns green, his hair shifts blue, and his eyes glow yellow. He should still look recognizably like Jet after the transformation.

## Zombie At The Door

An ordinary infected apartment visitor with gray-green decayed skin, torn casual clothes, slack jaw, milky eyes, and shambling posture. After Jet removes the head, the severed zombie head remains expressive, frightened, and weirdly alive.

## HudsonAlpha Scientist

One-scene supporting scientist in a lab coat or protective lab jacket, sprinting through a genetics laboratory hallway while carrying a glowing serum vial. The scientist is not established as recurring in this issue.

## Settings

Emerald Ridge Apartment is Jet's lived-in home office and routine space: desk with computer monitors and AI comic notes, modest furniture, pancake and milkshake routine traces, streaming microphone/camera, front door with peephole, and ordinary apartment lighting.

The street outside Emerald Ridge is an ordinary Huntsville apartment complex street with parked cars, sidewalks, low buildings, and trees, temporarily disrupted by slow zombies and armed survivors.

HudsonAlpha is represented as a high-tech Huntsville genetics laboratory with bright corridors, glass, lab benches, warning lights, serum vials, and a large emergency steam machine.

## Powers And Effects

Lucid Light is cosmic purple energy with star-like motes, translucent ribbons, and lens distortions. Failed Lucid Light should be shown as only a faint imagined purple glimmer or absent energy around Jet's hand.

A Lucid Conduit is represented in the prologue as an ordinary object with subtle purple cosmic light leaking from cracks or reflections.

The cure fog is glowing blue vapor spreading through streets and apartment interiors, returning zombies to normal.
