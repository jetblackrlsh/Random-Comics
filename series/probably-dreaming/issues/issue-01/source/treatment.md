# Probably Dreaming: Bite Me at 4:30

## Issue Number

1

## Logline

Jet Black assumes a zombie at his apartment door is proof that he is dreaming, so he becomes a zombie on purpose and treats the apocalypse as a boring Lucid Light problem until Huntsville mysteriously resets.

## Central Conflict

Jet wants to use dream logic and Lucid Light to explore what he thinks is a zombie dream, but he lacks Lucid Light, is trapped indoors by armed survivors, and must improvise around a frightened severed zombie head.

## Theme

Routine can make even the impossible feel manageable, but it can also make a person ignore evidence that reality is stranger than their explanation for it.

## Ending

A HudsonAlpha cure fog restores the city and Jet wakes up normal, convinced the experience was a dream. The severed zombie head remains in the corner, implying the adventure physically happened.

## Emotional Arc

Jet moves from amused certainty, to experimental curiosity, to boredom, to mild strategic concern, and finally back to casual denial.
