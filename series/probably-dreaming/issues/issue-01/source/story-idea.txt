Series Title:
Probably Dreaming

Issue Title:
Bite Me at 4:30

Series Premise:
Jet Black is a quirky and disciplined person who quickly assumes that anything weird happening around him means he is probably dreaming. In his dreams, he manipulates Lucid Light, a cosmic purple energy that allows him to manipulate the dream world. When Lucid Light is unavailable, he searches for a person, place, or thing containing Lucid Light called a Lucid Conduit. The series follows Jet through bizarre situations that may actually be happening, though Jet almost always writes them off as dreams after normalcy returns.

Issue One Premise:
Jet hears a knock at his apartment door while writing a new comic. When he sees a zombie through the peephole, he smiles, assumes he must be dreaming, opens the door, and tells the zombie to bite him. He lets the zombie infect his arm, removes its head to protect his brain, and turns into a zombie without feeling hungry for brains. Jet concludes that his one-meal-a-day routine means zombie hunger will not arrive until breakfast at 4:30 AM. Bored by the slow zombie apocalypse outside, he tries to summon Lucid Light. When it fails, he considers searching for a Lucid Conduit, but gunshots outside and a terrified severed zombie head force him to stay hidden. Survivors almost enter his apartment before leaving. At HudsonAlpha, a scientist releases glowing serum through a steam machine, spreading blue fog across the city and curing the zombies. Jet returns to normal, assumes it was all a dream, and misses the severed zombie head still sitting in the room.
