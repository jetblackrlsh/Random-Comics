# Character Descriptions

## Character: Jet Black

- First appears: Issue #1, "Bite Me at 4:30"
- Role: protagonist.
- Visual identifiers: Black man in his 30s with medium-brown skin, long black dreadlocks past his shoulders, full beard and mustache, warm smile, slim-to-average build, relaxed posture, black zip hoodie with white drawstrings, black shirt under the hoodie with visible colorful text reading "Dream Anime", black pants, white sneakers, small black crossbody/fanny pack worn across the front.
- Continuity notes: Disciplined work-from-home developer, AI-comics enthusiast, livestreamer, lucid dream adventurer. Assumes bizarre events are dreams and searches for Lucid Light or Lucid Conduits when his powers do not work.
