# Issue Summaries

## Issue #1: Bite Me at 4:30

- Folder: `series/probably-dreaming/issues/issue-01/`
- Logline: Jet Black assumes a zombie at his apartment door is proof that he is dreaming, becomes a bored zombie on purpose, then misses the evidence after Huntsville returns to normal.
- Plot summary: After a prologue introduces Jet's dream logic and Lucid Light rules, Jet hears insistent knocking while writing a comic. He sees a zombie through the peephole, decides he must be dreaming, and eagerly invites the bite. He carefully lets the zombie infect him without eating his brain, removes its head, and waits for zombie hunger that never arrives because his one-meal-a-day routine has him thinking breakfast is not until 4:30 AM. Bored by the slow zombie apocalypse outside, Jet tries to summon Lucid Light and realizes he may need a Lucid Conduit. Gunfire outside and a panicking severed zombie head force him to hide quietly from armed survivors. At HudsonAlpha, a scientist releases glowing serum through a steam machine, spreading blue fog across Huntsville and returning the zombies to normal. Jet wakes back into ordinary life and calls it a dream while failing to notice the severed zombie head still in his room.
- Continuity changes: The issue establishes Jet's daily routine, dream assumptions, Lucid Light, Lucid Conduits, and the series' pattern of normalcy returning while physical evidence remains.
- New characters: Jet Black.
- New settings: Emerald Ridge Apartment; Huntsville Street Outside Emerald Ridge.
- New key items: Lucid Light; Lucid Conduit.
