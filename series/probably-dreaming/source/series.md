# Probably Dreaming

## Logline

Jet Black treats every bizarre disruption in Huntsville like a lucid dream, calmly following his routines while searching for Lucid Light in situations that are strongly implied to be real.

## Premise

Jet Black is a quirky and disciplined software developer who assumes that anything weird happening around him probably means he is dreaming. In his dreams, he manipulates Lucid Light: cosmic purple energy that lets him reshape the dream world when the dream is flexible enough to respond. Not every dream starts with Lucid Light available, so Jet often searches for a Lucid Conduit, a person, place, or thing containing Lucid Light that lets him wield the power.

The series follows Jet as he finds himself in absurd situations around Huntsville, Alabama, especially at home in Emerald Ridge Apartments. Whether the adventures are dreams is never fully clear to Jet, but the stories strongly imply that they are actually happening. By the end, reality mostly returns to normal after Jet is knocked out, falls asleep, loses his memory, or otherwise misses the evidence. He writes off the experience as a dream and continues his day.

## Tone

Absurdist slice-of-life fantasy comedy. Jet reacts to impossible worlds with ordinary problem-solving, disciplined routines, and casual confidence. The humor comes from the contrast between escalating supernatural danger and Jet's oddly practical dream logic.

## Recurring Visual Language

- Realistic candid photo-comic style with documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism framing.
- Huntsville, Alabama locations should feel like ordinary real places interrupted by impossible dream logic.
- Emerald Ridge Apartments should feel lived-in, modest, and routine-focused: a work-from-home apartment, pancake breakfast details, streaming setup, AI comic notes, and quiet residential hallways.
- Lucid Light appears as cosmic purple energy with star-like motes, translucent ribbons, and gentle lens distortions.
- Lucid Conduits are ordinary-looking people, places, or objects with subtle purple cosmic light leaking from seams, reflections, or small surface details.
- Caption boxes only. No speech bubbles or thought bubbles.

## Continuity Notes

- Jet wakes up at 4:30 AM every day.
- Jet works from home as a software application developer and normally stops work at 2:00 PM to start his livestream.
- Jet eats pancakes every morning as his one meal a day.
- Jet drinks a chocolate peanut butter milkshake with many mix-ins every day at 9:45 AM.
- Jet is obsessed with AI tools and making AI comics.
- Jet spends nights adventuring in lucid dreams, and those dreams inspire many of his creations.
- Jet's first explanation for weird events is that he must be dreaming.
- Lucid Light is real within Jet's dream logic, but the series should leave room for the possibility that the bizarre events are physically happening.
- When Jet lacks Lucid Light, he searches for a Lucid Conduit instead of panicking.
- Each issue should include a prologue page that explains the basic series formula for new readers.
