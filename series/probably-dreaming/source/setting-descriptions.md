# Setting Descriptions

## Setting: Emerald Ridge Apartment

- First appears: Issue #1, "Bite Me at 4:30"
- Visual identifiers: modest Huntsville apartment interior with work-from-home desk, laptop and monitors, AI comic notes, pancake breakfast traces, streaming microphone or camera, simple couch, front door with peephole, quiet apartment hallway, ordinary residential lighting.
- Continuity notes: Jet's home base and primary routine space. Bizarre events often interrupt the normal apartment rhythm.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/emerald-ridge-apartment-setting.png`

## Setting: Huntsville Street Outside Emerald Ridge

- First appears: Issue #1, "Bite Me at 4:30"
- Visual identifiers: ordinary Alabama apartment complex street with parked cars, sidewalks, low apartment buildings, trees, humid daylight or dusk, and suburban-residential calm that can be disrupted by supernatural events.
- Continuity notes: Recurring threshold between Jet's controlled apartment routine and the bizarre adventures he assumes are dreams.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/huntsville-apartment-street-setting.png`
