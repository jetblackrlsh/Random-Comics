# Key Item Descriptions

## Key Item: Lucid Light

- First appears: Issue #1, "Bite Me at 4:30"
- Visual identifiers: cosmic purple energy with star-like motes, translucent ribbons, soft glow, lens distortions, and impossible reflections that look composited into candid real-world footage.
- Continuity notes: Jet believes he can manipulate dream worlds with Lucid Light. It does not always manifest at the start of a dream or bizarre event.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/lucid-light-key-item.png`

## Key Item: Lucid Conduit

- First appears: Issue #1, "Bite Me at 4:30"
- Visual identifiers: an otherwise ordinary object, person, or place containing subtle Lucid Light, usually visible as purple cosmic glow leaking from cracks, edges, reflections, or internal seams.
- Continuity notes: When Jet cannot access Lucid Light directly, he searches for a Lucid Conduit so he can wield dream-shaping power.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/lucid-conduit-key-item.png`
