Create Generic Man Issue #1 for the Random Comics series "Generic Man".

Series premise: Generic Man is the story of Genny Manson, a kind man with no Hacks in Super World, a bubble universe inside the Storyverse where everyone has force projection and almost everyone has extra powers called Hacks. Genny has completely mastered force projection, making him the most powerful force projection user in the world, but society and the Hero Association ignore him because they care only about unique Hacks. The series asks what makes someone special, whether being the best at something matters if no one values it, and whether a hero is defined by many tools or mastery of one tool.

Issue premise: Genny is scanned shortly after birth to determine his Hack ability. The scan returns negative, and his high-society parents abandon him for adoption. Genny grows up moving through foster homes, bullied by other children who use their Hacks against him. He admires helper robots because they help everyone without needing Hacks. He trains force projection by copying robots and drones: hardening his skin with a field, lifting heavy objects with invisible force, and clumsily propelling himself into flight. When his bullies confront him again, he protects himself, dodges, and knocks them back. They call him generic, but Genny accepts the word as a future badge of honor and imagines becoming a blank white hero who helps people whether or not anyone cares.
