# Page Script

All pages are full realistic candid photo-comic pages in 4:5 portrait aspect ratio. Caption-box text must be generated directly inside the page art. Use caption boxes only, no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating subtitles, no blank text boxes, and no later text overlay.

## Page 1: Cover

- Story role: Introduce Genny as a blank, ignored hero in a world obsessed with flashy Hacks.
- Layout: Single dramatic cover image with a slight documentary-photo feel.
- Visual beats: Future Generic Man in an all-white bodysuit and cape stands in the middle of a busy Super World plaza. Colorful heroes in custom Hacks suits stride past him without looking. Invisible force ripples bend dust around his hands and feet. His blank white mask faces the viewer, unreadable but gentle.
- Caption/title text: Cover title text: "Generic Man". Smaller issue title text: "Nothing Special".
- Prompt notes: Realistic superhero photo-comic cover, not painted poster art; readable title text generated inside the cover image.

## Page 2: Negative

- Story role: Establish Super World's rules and Genny's social wound at birth.
- Layout: Four panels: Super World city exterior, scan nursery, diagnostic result, parents turning away.
- Caption boxes: "In Super World, every child was born with force projection." "Almost every child had something extra." "They called the extras Hacks." "Genny Manson had none."
- Visual beats: Futuristic city full of Hacks suits and helper robots; newborn Genny in a transparent Hack scan crib under blue light; holographic readout showing a simple negative result; wealthy parents in elegant Hacks suits looking ashamed and angry while a technician holds a tablet.

## Page 3: The System

- Story role: Show abandonment, foster drift, and how society treats Genny as an anomaly instead of a child.
- Layout: Five panels with time passing through documents, rooms, and faces.
- Caption boxes: "His parents signed quickly." "The file followed him faster than love did." "Every home asked what his Hack would become." "Every answer was the same."
- Visual beats: Adoption form on a white desk; infant Genny handed to a foster intake worker; toddler Genny in a too-large jacket beside a service robot; new foster doorway; older child Genny sitting alone while other children compare glowing Hacks effects.

## Page 4: Helpers

- Story role: Connect bullying, Genny's loneliness, and his admiration for robots.
- Layout: Four panels: bullying, aftermath, robot kindness, Genny watching robots work.
- Caption boxes: "Other children made status into a weapon." "Their Hacks were small, but they still hurt." "The robots never asked what made him special." "They only helped."
- Visual beats: Bullies in mismatched Hacks suits shove young Genny with small power effects; Genny bruised beside the foster courtyard wall; a helper robot gently offers a repair cloth and snack pack; Genny watches robots lift crates and guide elderly citizens across a plaza.

## Page 5: Skin Of Force

- Story role: Begin Genny's training and show force projection as discipline.
- Layout: Training montage with five panels.
- Caption boxes: "So Genny practiced the power everyone ignored." "First, he learned to keep it close." "A shield did not need to shine." "It only needed to hold."
- Visual beats: Genny alone in the utility training yard, palms hovering near his arms; dust and rain bending around a skin-tight invisible field; thrown pebbles stopping against air ripples; Genny touching his forearm in amazement as pressure dents appear around him.

## Page 6: Lift And Fall

- Story role: Show Genny imitating construction robots and drones to expand force projection.
- Layout: Six quick panels with a clear progression from clumsy to capable.
- Caption boxes: "He watched machines move weight without pride." "He taught the air to become hands." "Flight came harder." "The ground kept correcting him."
- Visual beats: Construction robots moving heavy beams; Genny using invisible force to lift small blocks, then larger slabs; a drone rising smoothly overhead; Genny launching himself too fast and tumbling into safety mats; final panel shows teen Genny hovering awkwardly but smiling.

## Page 7: Generic

- Story role: Pay off the training when the bullies return.
- Layout: Five action panels.
- Caption boxes: "The bullies came back older." "Genny was still outnumbered." "But he was not unprepared." "The word they threw at him finally missed."
- Visual beats: Teen bullies surround Genny in the foster block courtyard with flashy small Hacks; attacks splash harmlessly across a skin-tight force field; Genny launches clumsily upward to dodge; he lands badly but channels invisible force through the ground; a clean pressure wave knocks the bullies backward without maiming them.

## Page 8: Badge Of Honor

- Story role: Resolve the issue by turning the insult into identity and foreshadowing the hero suit.
- Layout: Four panels ending in a future-vision splash.
- Caption boxes: "They ran calling him nothing special." "Generic, they said." "Genny smiled like the word belonged to him now." "One day, he would make it mean hero."
- Visual beats: Bullies fleeing down the courtyard stairs; Genny standing dusty and breathing hard, still kind instead of cruel; helper robot watching from a doorway as Genny looks at his hands; final wide future-vision panel of Generic Man in a blank white suit and cape lifting into the sky over Super World on invisible force ripples.
