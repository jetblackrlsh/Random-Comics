# Character Bible

This issue uses the series continuity files in `series/generic-man/source/` and the reference images in `series/generic-man/reference-images/`.

## Genny Manson / Generic Man

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/genny-manson-generic-man-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Tan brown skin, blue eyes, black hair with a distinct cowlick, lean athletic build, kind smile that hides loneliness. As a child he wears plain foster-system clothes and scuffed sneakers. During training he grows from small child to teen through montage panels but keeps the cowlick, blue eyes, and open expression. His future heroic identity wears an all-white bodysuit, white cape, and smooth blank white mask with no logo or detail.

## Helper Robots

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/helper-robots-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Humanoid civic-service robots with smooth white ceramic shells, soft blue sensor strips, practical utility hands, gentle posture, service scuffs, tool panels, and calm helpful body language.

## Genny's Birth Parents

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: None for Issue #1.
- Stable page identifiers: Wealthy Super World couple in elegant custom Hacks suits, polished armor panels, controlled expressions, anxious status-conscious body language. They should feel emotionally distant, not monstrous.

## Foster-Home Bullies

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: None for Issue #1.
- Stable page identifiers: Group of children and teens in secondhand colorful Hacks suits with mismatched armor plates and distinct small power effects. They appear confident when Genny is defenseless and frightened when his trained force projection finally pushes them back.

## Super World Citizens

- Stable page identifiers: Varied civilians in colorful Hacks suits, visible personalized power signatures, assisted by robots and drones. They treat Genny's lack of Hacks as an administrative fact rather than an emergency.

## Continuity Notes For Page Prompts

- Force projection is mostly invisible; show it through lensing, air ripples, dust rings, floating objects, pressure dents, and shimmering skin-tight fields.
- Genny's ability should never look like a flashy Hack. It should look plain, invisible, and practical until the results become impossible to ignore.
- Caption boxes are the only readable story text. Do not use speech bubbles, thought bubbles, floating subtitles, blank text boxes, or later text overlays.
