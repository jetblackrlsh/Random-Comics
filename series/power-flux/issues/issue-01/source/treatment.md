# Treatment

## Title

The Selfish Wish

## Issue Number

1

## Logline

Rann Dominique selfishly wishes for superpowers, unleashes an alien prison, dies, and returns from Limbo with unstable powers and one chance to save Earth.

## Central Conflict

Rann wants an extraordinary life for himself, but his wish creates a catastrophe for everyone else. In Limbo, he must accept that his new powers are not a prize. They are a sentence, a tool, and a test.

## Theme

Power cannot redeem selfishness unless it is used for someone else's sake.

## Ending

Rann returns to the crash field in Flux Armor with a dark halo and wildly shifting powers. Dark Angel watches from a Limbo gateway as reports of alien fugitives spread across the world. Rann takes his first frightened step toward becoming a hero.

## Emotional Arc

Rann moves from bored entitlement to selfish wonder, then terror, shame, bargaining, and reluctant acceptance. He is not selfless by the end, but he understands that survival and salvation now require him to become better than the man who made the wish.

## Story Shape

Eight pages: cover, Rann's ordinary dissatisfaction, the selfish wish, the alien prison crash, Limbo judgment, the blocked gateway to Hell, the resurrection bargain with shifting powers, and Rann's return to Earth as Power Flux.
