# Character Bible

All pages use realistic candid photo-comic style, documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism feel, natural imperfect framing, believable real-world lighting, photoreal action VFX, clean rectangular caption boxes, and no speech bubbles or thought bubbles.

## Rann Dominique

Adult man in his late 20s to early 30s, brown eyes, brown tan skin, short curly afro of black hair, expressive oval face, restless posture, and a tendency to look like he is waiting for the world to impress him. Before death he wears a dark casual jacket, gray T-shirt, jeans, and sneakers. After resurrection he wears Flux Armor: dark glossy black and gunmetal extradimensional armor with glowing question marks on the chest, shoulders, and gauntlets. A dark glittering halo floats above his head. His powers shift between panels and should look unstable rather than controlled.

Reference image: `../../../reference-images/rann-dominique-character.png`

## Dark Angel

Tall imposing angel of Limbo with ash-gray skin, severe face, dim gold eyes, black feathered wings with ember-like cracks, horn-like shadow shapes over the brow, dark robes layered over worn ceremonial armor, and a stern disappointed posture. He opens black-gold dimensional gateways and communicates from Limbo.

Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dark-angel-character.png`

## Flux Armor

Dark extradimensional battle armor given to Rann by Dark Angel. It has glossy black and gunmetal plates, a subtle rough Limbo-metal texture, and glowing question marks on the chest, shoulders, and gauntlets. The question marks change color with Rann's current power.

Reference image: `../../../reference-images/flux-armor-key-item.png`

## Alien Prison Rock

Meteor-like stone-metal prison with cratered black shell, embedded alien containment glyphs, blue-white cracks, broken capsule cavities, and prison-cell architecture fused into the rock. It is first seen as a falling star, then as cracked wreckage in a crater.

Reference image: `../../../reference-images/alien-prison-rock-key-item.png`

## Limbo Threshold

Endless pale fog, fractured black stone floor, floating doorways, distant stair shapes, black-gold cracks of light, a burning red gateway suggesting Hell, and a cold high white glow suggesting Heaven out of reach.

Reference image: `../../../reference-images/limbo-threshold-setting.png`

## Rann's Crash Field

Nighttime grassy field near a city edge with a scorched crater, emergency smoke, shattered stone-metal fragments, glowing containment cracks, broken alien cells, and distant city lights under a damaged sky.
