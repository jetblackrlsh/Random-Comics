Series Title:

Power Flux

Series Premise:

When selfish dreamer Rann Dominique wishes on a shooting star for superpowers, he accidentally unleashes an alien prison full of the galaxy's worst criminals and gets himself killed. Returned to life by a grim angel of Limbo, Rann is given unstable, constantly shifting powers and one chance at redemption: save Earth from the fugitives he released, or lose his soul to Hell. To survive, he must master impossible battles with abilities that change at random, but to be saved, he must become something far harder than powerful. He must become selfless.

Issue One Premise:

Rann Dominique saw a shooting star and made a selfish wish. Instead of wishing for peace on Earth, the end of world hunger, or anything else that could have helped his fellow man, he wished for superpowers. The shooting star plummeted out of the sky and struck Rann down. The shooting star was actually a space rock where some of the worst criminals from across the galaxy were exiled for eternity. The space rock explodes and releases those prisoners across the Earth. Rann's spirit is cast into Limbo. Dark Angel chastises him for making such a selfish wish, opens a gateway to Hell, and pushes Rann toward it. Rann bounces off an invisible force field. Dark Angel, both annoyed and relieved, informs Rann that the man upstairs is more forgiving than him. Dark Angel tells Rann that if he wants to avoid eternal suffering in Hell, he must save the planet with the superpowers he received from his wish. Rann did not specify what powers he wanted, so his powers shift randomly from moment to moment. Terrified of Hell, amazed by his powers, and wanting to live, Rann agrees to the terms and is rocketed from Limbo back to the land of the living. Armed with a suit of armor and a halo, he now travels the world as a wandering hero with Dark Angel as his guide, fighting alien menaces in search of redemption.
