# Setting Descriptions

## Setting: Limbo Threshold

- First appears: Issue #1, "The Selfish Wish"
- Visual identifiers: endless pale fog, fractured black stone floor, floating doorways, distant stair shapes, black-gold cracks of light, one burning red gateway suggesting Hell, and a colder high white glow suggesting Heaven far out of reach. The space feels photographed like a real abandoned terminal or quarry transformed by cosmic spiritual effects.
- Continuity notes: Rann's spirit arrives here after death. Dark Angel uses this threshold as a courtroom, transit station, and surveillance point for Earth.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/limbo-threshold-setting.png`

## Setting: Rann's Crash Field

- First appears: Issue #1, "The Selfish Wish"
- Visual identifiers: nighttime grassy field near a city edge, scorched crater, emergency smoke, shattered stone-metal fragments, glowing containment cracks, broken alien cells, and distant city lights under a damaged sky.
- Continuity notes: The alien prison rock strikes near Rann here, kills him, and releases fugitives across Earth.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/rann-crash-field-setting.png`
