# Issue Summaries

## Issue #1: The Selfish Wish

- Folder: `series/power-flux/issues/issue-01/`
- Logline: Rann Dominique selfishly wishes for superpowers, unleashes an alien prison, dies, and returns from Limbo with unstable powers and one chance to save Earth.
- Plot summary: Rann mistakes an alien prison rock for a shooting star and wishes for powers instead of anything that could help others. The rock crashes, kills him, and releases alien criminals across Earth. In Limbo, Dark Angel condemns Rann's selfishness and tries to push him into Hell, but an invisible mercy blocks the gate. Rann accepts a bargain: return to life with constantly shifting powers, guided by Dark Angel, and save the planet from the prisoners he freed.
- Continuity changes: Rann is resurrected with a dark halo, Flux Armor, and unstable powers. Dark Angel becomes his watcher and guide. The alien prisoners are loose across Earth.
- New characters: Rann Dominique, Dark Angel.
- New settings: Limbo Threshold, Rann's Crash Field.
- New key items: Alien Prison Rock, Flux Armor.
