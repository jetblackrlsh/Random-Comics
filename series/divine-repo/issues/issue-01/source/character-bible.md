## Recurring Characters

### Adam Frankenstein

- Use series entry: `Character: Adam Frankenstein`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/adam-frankenstein-character.png`
- Issue state: Exhausted, injured, and practical. He wears dark work clothes under Second Skin. His white temple streak, gray-green eyes, stubble, and scarred hands must remain consistent.

### Second Skin

- Use series entry: `Character: Second Skin`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/second-skin-character.png`
- Issue state: Damaged but functional. Black biomechanical armor with silver liquid seams, violet cracks, and matter-shaping tendrils. Its indicator lights flicker between confident blue and unstable amber.

### God

- Use series entry: `Character: God`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/god-lucid-dreamer-character.png`
- Issue state: Confused but transactional. He appears as a rich comatose man and as a dream god inside a hospital-casino penthouse, surrounded by ledgers and purple cosmic light.

### Immortal Curse

- Use series entry: `Character: Immortal Curse`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/immortal-curse-character.png`
- Issue state: Recently adapted to Second Skin's atomic manipulation. It should look wet, pale-gray, half-human, and constantly reshaping.

## Issue-Specific Character

### Brother Prosper Vale

- Role: one-issue antagonist, fake miracle preacher, first Lucid Light debtor.
- Visual identifiers: Charismatic middle-aged man with copper-brown skin, manic smile, slick black hair, white-and-gold preacher suit, rings on every finger, purple glow under his eyes and palms, microphone headset, theatrical stage posture.
- Continuity notes: He borrowed Lucid Light and turned it into a prosperity cult that multiplies donations and stages healings. He is not recorded as a recurring element unless a later issue brings him back.
