Series Title: Divine Repo

Issue #1 Title: Past Due Miracle

Adam Frankenstein has just learned the worst possible truth: Second Skin, the thought-reactive AI symbiote suit he built to finally kill the Immortal Curse, has failed. The adaptive protoplasmic hunter survived and is now immune to the suit's atomic matter manipulation.

Adam searches the family records for any force the Curse has never adapted to and finds a reference to God's Lucid Light, a purple dream-born power capable of rewriting reality. God still exists, but only as a confused former billionaire trapped in a medically induced lucid coma. God admits he loaned most of the Lucid Light to thieves, frauds, monsters, cult leaders, and would-be superheroes who took the power and stopped paying.

God offers Adam a deal: repossess the missing miracles, and Adam can keep enough recovered Lucid Light to build a final weapon against the Curse. The first debtor is Brother Prosper Vale, a prosperity-cult preacher who uses stolen Lucid Light to multiply donations and stage miracles inside a packed neon chapel.

Adam and Second Skin infiltrate the miracle service, refuse to hurt the believers, and collect the stolen divine spark directly from Prosper's altar. The Immortal Curse follows Adam to the job, but the recovered Lucid Light blinds and repels it for a few seconds, proving the power can still affect what Second Skin cannot.

Adam leaves with the first spark, the Divine Ledger, and a terrible new job: collect every unpaid miracle before the Curse adapts again.
