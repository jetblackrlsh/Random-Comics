# Setting Descriptions

## Setting: Frankenstein Bunker Lab

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Visual identifiers: Underground concrete laboratory beneath a ruined family estate, wet stone walls, hanging surgical lights, old brass Frankenstein instruments mixed with modern servers, scorched containment rings, handwritten family kill-ledgers pinned beside monitors, emergency red lighting, narrow exits reinforced with steel shutters.
- Continuity notes: Adam's inherited workshop and survival shelter. The room carries generations of failed anti-Curse inventions and becomes Adam's staging ground for divine repo work.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/frankenstein-bunker-lab-setting.png`

## Setting: God's Lucid Coma Penthouse

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Visual identifiers: Dreamlike penthouse hospital suite floating inside purple cosmic clouds, luxury casino carpet, gold fixtures, medical coma bed, IV lines, heart monitor, stacks of ledgers, floating contract pages, windows looking into impossible stars, money motifs warped by dream logic.
- Continuity notes: God's mind presents his coma as a luxury penthouse and casino office. It is both a hospital room and a divine lending office where unpaid miracle accounts appear as glowing records.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/gods-lucid-coma-penthouse-setting.png`
