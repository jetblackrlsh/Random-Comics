# Key Item Descriptions

## Key Item: Lucid Light

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Visual identifiers: Purple cosmic energy that looks like liquid starlight, dream dust, glassy refraction, and faint rectangular contract sigils. It glows through skin, machines, money, weapons, and architecture when misused by debtors.
- Continuity notes: God's dream-born power. Borrowers use it to rewrite their lives and reality, but Adam needs to collect enough of it to attempt a final weapon against the Immortal Curse.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/lucid-light-key-item.png`

## Key Item: Divine Ledger

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Visual identifiers: Heavy black account book with cracked leather cover, gold-edged pages, purple glowing balance columns, impossible moving ink, divine contract tabs, and fingerprints burned into the margins like audit marks.
- Continuity notes: God's record of Lucid Light debtors. It identifies who stole divine power, how much they owe, and where Adam can collect. The ledger becomes Adam's roadmap for the series.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/divine-ledger-key-item.png`
