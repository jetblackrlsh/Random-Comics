# Character Descriptions

## Character: Adam Frankenstein

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Role: protagonist, cursed scientist, reluctant divine repo man.
- Visual identifiers: Man in his early 30s with pale olive skin, lean exhausted build, sharp cheekbones, gray-green eyes, messy black hair with a white streak at the temple, stubble, faint old burn scars on his hands and neck, dark work clothes under a damaged black symbiote suit. His posture is tense and practical, like someone who sleeps near exits.
- Continuity notes: Raised to become a mad scientist because invention is how the Frankenstein line survives. He built Second Skin as the ultimate weapon against the Immortal Curse, but the Curse adapted to it. He accepts God's repo work because Lucid Light may be the only remaining untested weapon.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/adam-frankenstein-character.png`

## Character: Second Skin

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Role: Adam's AI symbiote suit, partner, weapon, anxious conscience.
- Visual identifiers: Damaged black biomechanical suit bonded over Adam's body, matte polymer surfaces, liquid silver seams, violet diagnostic cracks, thin matter-shaping tendrils that unfold from the forearms and spine, collar-like neck interface, half-helmet that can retract or form around Adam's face. When speaking through visual cues, it flickers between confident clean blue indicators and unstable amber error lights.
- Continuity notes: Thought-reactive and capable of atomic matter manipulation, but no longer able to defeat the Immortal Curse. Its AI alternates between overconfident encouragement and despair about its failure.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/second-skin-character.png`

## Character: God

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Role: confused dream god, former rich man, lender of Lucid Light.
- Visual identifiers: Heavyset older man with warm brown skin, silver hair, trimmed silver beard, expensive silk robe over hospital monitors and IV lines, gold jewelry, bare feet, tired eyes, and a smile that cannot decide whether it is generous or calculating. In dream form he appears inside purple cosmic casino light with floating account books and medical cables fading into clouds.
- Continuity notes: Once a very rich thrill seeker. He entered a medically induced lucid coma to enjoy the ultimate extreme, forgot he was dreaming, and began loaning Lucid Light for a percentage of other people's earnings. Almost every borrower stole the power and vanished.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/god-lucid-dreamer-character.png`

## Character: Immortal Curse

- First appears: Issue #1, "Past Due Miracle"
- Role: adaptive immortal killer, generational antagonist.
- Visual identifiers: Tall amorphous humanoid mass of translucent pale-gray slime, uneven shoulders, half-formed skull shape under wet membrane, black sensory pits where eyes should be, bone-white nodules embedded like failed teeth, long arms that reshape into hooks, blades, and blunt clubs, constant dripping trail, skin texture like living protoplasm over scar tissue.
- Continuity notes: Created by Victor Frankenstein, rejected immediately, and conditioned by violence. It exists to kill Frankenstein. It cannot be permanently stopped by the same method twice and has already adapted to Second Skin's matter manipulation.
- Reference image: `../reference-images/immortal-curse-character.png`
