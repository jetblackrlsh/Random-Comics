# Issue Summaries

## Issue #1: Past Due Miracle

- Folder: `series/divine-repo/issues/issue-01/`
- Logline: After Second Skin fails against the Immortal Curse, Adam Frankenstein takes God's first repo job and reclaims stolen Lucid Light from a fake miracle preacher.
- Plot summary: A Who's Who dossier introduces Adam Frankenstein, Second Skin, God, and the Immortal Curse. Adam survives another attack by the Immortal Curse and learns that his best invention, Second Skin, can no longer destroy it. In God's lucid coma penthouse, a confused dream god explains that the purple Lucid Light capable of ending the Curse has been loaned to thieves and frauds who skipped out on repayment. Adam tracks Brother Prosper Vale, a prosperity-cult preacher who uses stolen Lucid Light to multiply donations and stage miracles. Adam repossesses the miracle without killing the crowd, blinds the approaching Immortal Curse with the recovered spark, and accepts that he must collect every unpaid miracle before the Curse adapts again.
- Continuity changes: Adam becomes God's repo man. Second Skin remains damaged but recommits to helping Adam. God gives Adam the Divine Ledger. Adam recovers the first small spark of Lucid Light, proving the work can buy time but not yet victory.
- New characters: Adam Frankenstein, Second Skin, God, Immortal Curse.
- New settings: Frankenstein Bunker Lab, God's Lucid Coma Penthouse.
- New key items: Lucid Light, Divine Ledger.
