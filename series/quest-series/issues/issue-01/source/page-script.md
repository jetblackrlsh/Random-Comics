## Page 3: Graves In The Shit Water

- Story role: Establish Wes and Genevieve's final conversation before she leaves.
- Layout: Four panels: swamp graves, Genevieve holding back truth, Wes clinging to the story, Genevieve walking away.
- Caption boxes: "Genevieve came to say goodbye." "The truth sat behind her teeth." "Wes still believed Phil was sleeping." "So she left him one mercy and one wound."
- Visual beats: Wes and Genevieve kneel by two crude grave markers in The Shit Water; Genevieve looks at Wes with guilt; Wes touches a small carved sleeping-god charm; Genevieve walks toward a waiting husband and wagon while Wes stays at the graves.

## Page 4: The Fear That Answered

- Story role: Show Wes alone and the birth of his personal demon.
- Layout: Five panels growing darker from grief to supernatural manipulation.
- Caption boxes: "Alone, Wes listened for his parents." "The swamp listened back." "Fear learned his name." "It told him Phil could still make them proud." "Wes believed it."
- Visual beats: Wes sits in the empty family cabin at night; black water ripples outside without wind; smoke and mud form a gaunt horned shape behind him; the demon bends close like a whisper; Wes grips the carved charm and looks toward the wilderness.

## Page 5: The Road To Phil

- Story role: Show Wes gathering companions by promising salvation.
- Layout: Four travel panels across different wilderness locations.
- Caption boxes: "Wes crossed the world with a child's promise." "He told the hungry Phil would feed them." "He told the frightened Phil would guard them." "Some followed because hope was lighter than truth."
- Visual beats: Wes speaking beside a forest campfire to strangers; Wes helping travelers through a flooded road; Wes preaching with the demon hidden in his shadow; a small adventuring party walking behind him through mountains and scrubland.

## Page 6: Sister And Prophet

## Page 7: The Edge Of The World

## Page 8: Outside Reality

## Page 9: Phil Has Awoken

- Story role: Resolve the issue with Wes's mistaken joy and the world's catastrophe.
- Layout: Four panels ending on a world-scale horror image.
- Caption boxes: "Wes returned laughing." "He told them Phil had awakened." "Across the world, magic woke screaming." "Above it all, solitude learned rage."
