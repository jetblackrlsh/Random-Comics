Series Title: Quest Series

Series Creator: Aidan Miller
AI Artist: Nicholas Alexander Benson

Series Premise:
Wes, a young man who believes fairy tales his parents told him in the swamp, is tricked by a demon to travel across the world to wake up a god that does not exist. When he finally finds what he believes to be the god, he awakens a cosmic horror that threatens to destroy the world while also awakening magic potential across the planet. This series follows Wes as he is forced to grow up and put away childish beliefs in order to destroy his connection to the god and save his world.

Genre:
Character driven, coming of age, medieval fantasy, drama, adventure, cosmic horror.

Issue One Premise:
Genevieve leaves Wes behind in The Shit Water after one final talk by their parents' grave. Alone, Wes manifests a personal demon from his vulnerability and fear. The demon deepens his belief in Phil, the sleeping god from his parents' fairy tales, and pushes him to gather followers across the world. Years later, Genevieve confronts Wes after he has become a quest leader. Their reunion ends bitterly. Wes finally reaches The Hole, leaps into it, and brushes against a cosmic horror. Magic erupts across the world, and Wes believes Phil has awoken.
