# Treatment

## Title

The God That Wasn't

## Series

Quest Series

## Issue Number

1

## Creator Credits

- Series creator: Aidan Miller

## Logline

After his sister leaves the swamp, Wes follows a demon-fed fairy tale to the edge of reality and mistakes a cosmic horror's awakening for divine salvation.

## Central Conflict

Wes needs emotional safety after losing his parents and sister, but the only story that comforts him is false. Genevieve knows the truth but cannot make him accept it. Wes's Demon turns that gap into a quest that endangers the whole world.

## Theme

Growing up means learning that a comforting story can still harm you if you use it to avoid truth, grief, and responsibility.

## Emotional Arc

Wes begins as a grieving young man clinging to childhood faith. He becomes a charismatic wanderer whose belief gives frightened people hope. When Genevieve challenges him, he chooses certainty over family. By the end, he receives a terrifying sign that seems to prove him right, but the reader sees that his joy has unleashed catastrophe.

## Ending

Wes returns from The Hole radiant with relief, telling his followers that Phil has awakened. Across the world, uncontrolled magic rips through nature and the Cosmic Horror looms above reality, preparing to destroy the world that intruded on its solitude.
