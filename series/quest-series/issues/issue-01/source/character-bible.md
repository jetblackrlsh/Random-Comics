## Wes

Wes is an early-20s swamp-raised medieval traveler with a lean wiry build, pale olive skin, messy dark brown hair, open intense eyes, and a weathered innocent face. He wears a rough-spun tunic, patched dark green cloak, mud-stained boots, rope belt, and small travel satchel. Across the issue he ages from raw grief into a road-worn pilgrim-leader with the same eyes and cloak.

## Genevieve

Genevieve is Wes's older sister, late 20s to early 30s, with a composed but tired face, dark auburn hair tied back, practical medieval city dress, and a weathered traveling cloak. Her expression should carry love, guilt, and exhaustion. In later pages she appears cleaner, more settled, and more city-shaped, but not cold.

## Wes's Demon

Wes's Demon is a fear beast thoughtform: gaunt, shadowy, mud-black, with branch-like horns or antlers, ember-pinpoint eyes, long clawed fingers, and a body that can half-form from swamp mist, reeds, grave dirt, candle smoke, or Wes's shadow. It should feel like a hallucination made physically dangerous.

## Cosmic Horror

The Cosmic Horror is a higher-dimensional being, never fully shown. It appears as impossible scale, black negative space, wrong angles, pale luminous cracks, overlapping eyes or geometry, and reality-bending silhouettes beyond the sky or the edges of The Hole.

## Core Settings

The Shit Water is a desolate black-brown swamp with rotten reeds, crooked trees, crude graves, collapsing walkways, and damp gray light. The Wilderness is a sequence of harsh medieval frontier routes. The City is dense, crowded, stone-built, and human. The Hole is a bottomless black pit at the edge of the world where reality bends.
