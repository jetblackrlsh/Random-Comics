# Character Bible

This issue uses the series continuity files in `series/the-three-hands/source/` and the reference images in `series/the-three-hands/reference-images/`.

## King Kaine Chester

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/kaine-chester-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Black man, early 30s to early 40s, black buzzcut haircut, strong regal posture, crimson battle suit with gold accents, purple regal cheetah-print cape, controlled but haunted expression, stable glittering purple Lucid Light glowing from within.
- Issue state: Designs the King's Three Hands selection process after admitting that raw strength is not enough. He refuses to draft unwilling citizens and treats trust as the deciding criterion because he once controlled the people he now asks to protect him.

## Blake Hollister, the Black Hand

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/blake-hollister-black-hand-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Black man, bald, black battle suit, disciplined close-combat stance, restrained expression, compact defensive posture, heavy forearm guards, controlled black gravitational distortion around his hands only when needed.
- Issue state: Chosen because soldiering is central to his identity, because hand-to-hand combat lets him act precisely around Kaine, and because his black-hole power can bend and consume light-based bodies.

## Buster Green, the Green Hand

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/buster-green-hand-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Black man with spiky brown hair in a green battle suit, restless posture, tight jaw, bright green life-energy glow around his hands and shoulders, occasionally using slow breathing or meditation beads to steady himself.
- Issue state: Chosen because remaining a soldier gives him a constructive channel for his temper, and because his self-generated green light can strike Anamorphis bodies with the same luminous substance they are made from.

## Amani Rory, the Red Hand

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/amani-rory-red-hand-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Man with spiky black hair in a red battle suit, relaxed or sleepy posture, half-lidded eyes, red armored gauntlets that can reshape into melee weapons, casual efficiency under pressure.
- Issue state: Chosen because his laziness makes him brutally efficient, his soldier role gives him more downtime than any other job would, and his gauntlets can form hardlight blades and blunt weapons capable of damaging simulated Anamorphis bodies.

## Anamorphis Soldiers and Hardlight Simulations

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/anamorphis-soldier-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Faceless luminous humanoids, smooth featureless heads, bright energy bodies, force-projection flight trails, shapeshifting limbs, controlled glow intensity, super-speed blur. Hardlight simulations copy this silhouette but look slightly more geometric, translucent, and training-room artificial.
- Issue state: The simulation room can imitate basic Anamorphis movement and light bodies for candidate testing, but the hardlight copies are clearly weaker and less adaptive than a real Anamorphis soldier.

## Other Candidate Knights

- Source entry: issue-only supporting candidates.
- Reference image: None.
- Stable page identifiers: A varied group of unnamed superhuman citizens in colorful battle suits, each visually distinct by power effect: gravity armor, storm gloves, stone skin, sonic rings, magnetized metal, heat shimmer, and dreamlike plant growth.
- Issue state: They demonstrate why incredible raw power is not enough. Some are not knights, some cannot affect light, and some no longer want to serve after Kaine's old coercion.

## Kaine's Royal Terrace

- Source entry: `../../../source/setting-descriptions.md`

## Royal Hardlight Simulation Room

- Source entry: `../../../source/setting-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/royal-hardlight-simulation-room-setting.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Palace training chamber beneath the royal terrace, dark volcanic stone walls, white-and-gold projector pylons, transparent safety shields, purple command consoles, hardlight grid floor, palm-shadowed skylights, artificial Anamorphis silhouettes made from geometric white-blue light.
- Issue state: Used for candidate testing and combat training. The simulations are useful but limited; they are not as strong, adaptive, or dangerous as real Anamorphis soldiers.

## Lucid Light

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-light-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Glittering purple cosmic energy, star motes, crystalline ribbons, dreamlike lensing around hands and eyes, precise geometric arcs that can bend reality.

## Hardlight Training Simulators

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/hardlight-training-simulators-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: White-and-gold projector pylons with purple crystal lenses, transparent safety rails, floating diagnostic panes, controlled white-blue hardlight constructs shaped like simplified Anamorphis soldiers, visible warning markers that show training intensity.
- Issue state: They can imitate basic light-body targets for candidate testing and combat training, but cannot reproduce the full intelligence, strength, or unique techniques of real Anamorphis soldiers.
