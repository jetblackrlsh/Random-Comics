# Page Script

All pages are full realistic candid photo-comic pages in 4:5 portrait aspect ratio. Caption-box text must be generated directly inside the page art. Use caption boxes only, no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating subtitles, no blank text boxes, and no later text overlay.

## Page 1: Cover

- Story role: Present the issue as a tactical cosmic hunt and introduce the Lucid Dreamer versus Lucid Conduit distinction.
- Layout: Single dramatic cover image with documentary superhero-photo framing.
- Caption/title text: Cover title text: "The Three Hands". Smaller issue title text: "The Battery Problem".
- Visual beats: Dark Light stands in a rigid crystalline command chamber, faceless rainbow body wrapped in black aura, studying floating bubble-world projections. One projection shows a frightened Lucid Dreamer flickering away; another shows King Kaine Chester glowing with stable purple Lucid Light. An Anamorphis soldier waits beside a black-rainbow gateway.
- Prompt notes: Readable title text generated inside the cover image. Realistic candid photo-comic cover, not painted poster art.

## Page 2: One Soldier Per World

- Story role: Explain Dark Light's methodical deployment strategy.
- Layout: Four panels: command platform, gateway dispatch, failed soldier report, Dark Light studying the failure.
- Caption boxes: "Dark Light did not flood worlds with armies." "He sent one soldier." "If that soldier failed, the failure became data." "Then he chose the next blade."
- Visual beats: Dark Light on the Rigid Anamorphis Homeworld points to a bubble-universe projection. One faceless luminous Anamorphis soldier steps through the Storyverse Gateway alone. A damaged soldier returns as light fragments. Dark Light studies floating tactical diagrams with cold stillness while researchers stand back.

## Page 3: What Every Soldier Carries

- Story role: Define the shared Anamorphis powers and the unique special-technique rule.
- Layout: Five clean demonstration panels like a tactical field manual.
- Caption boxes: "Every Anamorphis carried the same grammar." "Force projection." "Glow control." "Shapeshifting." "Speed." "Technique made them dangerous."
- Visual beats: A soldier flies by force projection and knocks debris aside without touching it; another dims from bright white to a stealthy ember; another reshapes arms into luminous blades and shields; another becomes a speed blur; final panel shows three soldiers using unique techniques: a force spear, an invisible speed strike, and a shapeshifted capture net.

## Page 4: Worlds Without The Target

- Story role: Show many deployments failing because Lucid Lord is already gone.
- Layout: Four panels across different story worlds.
- Caption boxes: "Most doors opened on the wrong story." "Lucid Lord had already moved." "Some worlds never knew what hunted them." "Dark Light still recorded the absence."
- Visual beats: An Anamorphis soldier searches an empty noir rain alley filled with purple afterglow; another lands in a bright fairy-tale market and finds only a fading warning sigil; another scans a desert robot battlefield where Lucid Lord's trail has gone cold; final panel shows Dark Light receiving the negative reports as orderly points of light.

## Page 5: Dreamers Who Could Fight

- Story role: Stage the main action montage between Anamorphis soldiers and Lucid Dreamers.
- Layout: Six fast action panels with varied worlds and combat outcomes.
- Caption boxes: "Then some worlds answered back." "Lucid Dreamers bent sleep into weapons." "The docile ones broke quickly." "The practiced ones made soldiers bleed light." "Dark Light mistook resistance for nourishment."
- Visual beats: A frightened Lucid Dreamer in pajamas shields themself with unstable purple light while a soldier's force spear shatters the dream wall; a combat-ready Lucid Dreamer reshapes a floating city street into stairs and traps; an Anamorphis soldier uses glow control to blind a dreamer; another uses a shapeshifted net to catch a dream combatant; one soldier is hurled through a bubble-world castle window by a dreamer's purple shockwave.

## Page 6: No Morsel

- Story role: Reveal why captured Lucid Dreamers cannot feed Dark Light.
- Layout: Four panels: prisoner brought in, siphon begins, dreamer wakes, Dark Light left empty-handed.
- Caption boxes: "The prisoners looked like batteries." "Pain made the dream remember the body." "The dreamer woke." "And the Storyverse held nothing."
- Visual beats: A captured Lucid Dreamer kneels in a crystalline siphon circle before Dark Light, unstable purple light around their hands. Dark Light reaches with black-rainbow aura to drain the power. The dreamer's body turns transparent and snaps away like a waking reflection. The siphon chamber is left with only fading purple sparks and Dark Light's still silhouette.

## Page 7: The Researchers' Answer

- Story role: Explain Lucid Conduits and why Dark Light changes targets.
- Layout: Five panels: researchers present data, compare dreamer and conduit, show Lucid Lord, show Kaine, Dark Light decides.
- Caption boxes: "His researchers removed hope from the question." "Dreamers were visitors." "Conduits were native." "Native light did not wake away." "Dark Light finally knew what hunger required."
- Visual beats: Anamorphis researchers on the rigid homeworld project diagrams of a flickering Lucid Dreamer beside a stable Lucid Conduit. Lucid Lord appears as a purple living conduit in one projection. Kaine appears in another, crimson suit and cheetah-print cape, stable purple light rooted in his chest and hands. Dark Light leans toward Kaine's image with absolute focus.

## Page 8: No Abandoned Conduits

- Story role: End with dread: Lucid Lord's flight, Kaine's danger, and Dark Light's permanent obsession with Conduits.
- Layout: Four panels ending on a broad cosmic threat image.
- Caption boxes: "Lucid Lord knew the rule first." "A discovered Conduit was never abandoned." "Kaine's light was not borrowed." "When Dark Light found it, the hunt would become forever."
- Visual beats: Lucid Lord races through the Dream Realm Boundary, torn purple light trailing, leaving warnings in bubble universes. Kaine stands on his royal terrace, unaware that his stable Lucid Light marks him from within. Dark Light watches Kaine's world appear among bubble projections. Final panel: a single Anamorphis soldier steps toward the gateway, alone, chosen with methodical care.
