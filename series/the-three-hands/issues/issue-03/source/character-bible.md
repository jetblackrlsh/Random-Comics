# Character Bible

This issue uses the series continuity files in `series/the-three-hands/source/` and the reference images in `series/the-three-hands/reference-images/`.

## Dark Light

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dark-light-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Tall faceless humanoid made of rainbow luminous energy, smooth alien silhouette, no visible eyes or mouth, crackling black corruptive aura shrouding the body, analytical stillness. He should look like a ruler and strategist, not a frantic monster.
- Issue state: Deploys soldiers through the Storyverse, studies failed hunts, attempts to consume captured Lucid Dreamer power, then accepts the researchers' conclusion that only Lucid Conduits can satisfy him.

## The Lucid Lord

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-lord-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Humanoid being made entirely of glittering purple Lucid Light, translucent cosmic body, calm faceless luminous head, futuristic crystal rainbow sword of healing, golden energy tether coiled at one hand.
- Issue state: Constantly on the run through the Dream Realm Boundary, leaving warnings in worlds before Dark Light's soldiers arrive. He knows Dark Light will never abandon a discovered Lucid Conduit.

## King Kaine Chester

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/kaine-chester-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Black man, early 30s to early 40s, black buzzcut haircut, strong regal posture, crimson battle suit with gold accents, purple regal cheetah-print cape, controlled but haunted expression, stable purple Lucid Light glowing from within.
- Issue state: Appears as the final example of a native Lucid Conduit. He does not yet see the full scale of Dark Light's hunt, but the reader understands why his world will become a priority target.

## Anamorphis Soldiers

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/anamorphis-soldier-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Faceless luminous humanoid soldiers, smooth featureless heads, bright energy bodies, force-projection flight trails, shapeshifting limbs, controlled glow intensity, super-speed blur.
- Issue state: Deployed one per world to hunt Lucid Lord. Each uses the same core Anamorphis powers differently: one forms a spear from projected force, one dims into near-invisibility before a speed strike, one spreads shapeshifted limbs into a capture net.

## Lucid Dreamers

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-dreamer-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Human dream visitors wearing casual sleepwear, street clothes, or dream-shaped improvised outfits, unstable purple Lucid Light around hands and shoulders, translucent body edges, startled or combat-ready expressions.
- Issue state: Some are docile and overwhelmed by Anamorphis force. Others are experienced dream combatants who reshape battlefields and make soldiers adapt. All vanish from the Storyverse if the pain of siphoning wakes them.

## Lucid Conduits

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-conduit-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Native Storyverse beings or objects radiating stable glittering purple Lucid Light from within, with rooted star motes and precise geometric arcs. Their glow should feel anchored rather than borrowed.
- Issue state: The researchers identify Lucid Conduits as the real answer to Dark Light's hunger. Lucid Lord and Kaine are the issue's living examples.

## Rigid Anamorphis Homeworld

- Source entry: `../../../source/setting-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/rigid-anamorphis-homeworld-setting.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Monumental crystalline city, pale glass towers, strict symmetry, hard white light, black-rainbow banners, central palace platform, orderly Anamorphis soldiers and researchers.

## Dream Realm Boundary

- Source entry: `../../../source/setting-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dream-realm-boundary-setting.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Cosmic purple space filled with translucent bubble universes, each reflecting a different story-world, luminous paths between bubbles, nebula mist, floating islands and stars suspended in glassy spheres.

## Anamorphis Storyverse Gateway

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/anamorphis-storyverse-gateway-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Tall ring-shaped portal with white crystalline ribs, black-rainbow energy seams, bubble-universe reflections inside the opening, soldiers stepping through in streaks of light.
