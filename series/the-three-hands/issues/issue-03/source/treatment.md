# Treatment

## Title

The Battery Problem

## Issue Number

3

## Logline

Dark Light's one-soldier-at-a-time hunt across the Storyverse exposes the difference between temporary Lucid Dreamers and rare native Lucid Conduits, turning King Kaine Chester into an unavoidable target.

## Central Conflict

Dark Light needs a stable source of Lucid Light, but his soldiers keep finding dream visitors whose power disappears the moment fear and pain wake them. The Lucid Dreamers fight for survival inside worlds they barely understand, while Dark Light's researchers prove that only Lucid Conduits like Lucid Lord and Kaine can truly feed his hunger.

## Theme

A methodical predator becomes most dangerous when he learns exactly what not to chase.

## Ending

Dark Light stops wasting strategy on Lucid Dreamers and narrows his war to Lucid Conduits. Lucid Lord keeps fleeing from story to story because he understands the rule before Kaine does: once Dark Light confirms Kaine's nature, the hunt will not end.

## Emotional Arc

Dark Light begins as an irritated commander sorting through failed searches, becomes briefly intrigued by Lucid Dreamers, then grows colder and more certain when their captured power vanishes. The issue moves from chaotic multiverse battles to clinical revelation, ending with dread as Kaine's stable Lucid Light is identified as the kind of prize Dark Light can actually consume.

## Story Shape

Eight pages: cover, Dark Light's deployment method, Anamorphis power taxonomy, failed searches, Lucid Dreamer battles, the siphon failure, the researchers' answer, and a final warning that Kaine is a Lucid Conduit.
