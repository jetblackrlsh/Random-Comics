# Treatment

## Title

The Promise of Lucid Light

## Issue Number

1

## Logline

King Kaine Chester's island city nears rebellion under his corrupted Lucid Light rule until the Lucid Lord cleanses him and warns that a greater darkness is coming for his power.

## Central Conflict

Kaine believes control is the only way to keep his dream-born city safe. His people believe freedom is worth rebellion. The Lucid Lord arrives between them and forces Kaine to face the truth: protection without consent is tyranny.

## Theme

Power becomes honorable only when it accepts limits.

## Ending

After being cleansed, Kaine kneels before the citizens he once controlled, vows never again to use Lucid Light against them, and announces that he will form a team to protect the city without betraying its freedom.

## Emotional Arc

Kaine moves from proud certainty to panic, then shame, then public accountability. The city moves from fear and anger to cautious hope. The Lucid Lord remains calm throughout, acting as healer rather than conqueror.

## Story Shape

Eight pages: cover, city under control, rebellion rising, Lucid Lord's arrival, Kaine's failed domination attempt, cleansing battle, warning of Dark Light, and Kaine's public vow.
