# Character Bible

This issue uses the series continuity files in `series/the-three-hands/source/` and the reference images in `series/the-three-hands/reference-images/`.

## King Kaine Chester

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/kaine-chester-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Black man with black buzzcut haircut, crimson battle suit with gold accents, purple regal cheetah-print cape, royal posture. Before cleansing, his eyes and hands flare with sharp purple Lucid Light and his expression is controlling and severe. After cleansing, his posture softens and his expression becomes remorseful but resolute.

## The Lucid Lord

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-lord-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Humanoid being made of glittering purple Lucid Light, translucent cosmic body, calm luminous face, futuristic crystal rainbow sword, golden energy tether. The sword must read as healing, not lethal. The tether appears as braided golden light.

## Dark Light

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dark-light-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Tall faceless humanoid made of rainbow luminous energy, shrouded in crackling black corruptive aura. Appears only in the Lucid Lord's warning vision in this issue.

## Anamorphis Soldier

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/anamorphis-soldier-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Faceless luminous humanoid alien with smooth featureless head, energy-body limbs, flight posture, shapeshifting blur. Appears only in the warning vision in this issue.

## Citizens of the Island City

- Stable page identifiers: Superhuman island citizens in colorful battle suits, varied ages and body types, tropical streetwear mixed with armored panels. During Kaine's corrupted reign they move unnaturally in synchronized poses, then regain individual movement after the cleansing.

## Future Three Hands

Blake Hollister, Buster Green, and Amani Rory are documented in the series character descriptions and reference images but should not appear in the story pages for Issue #1.
