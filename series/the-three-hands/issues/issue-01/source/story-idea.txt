Create a new series called "The Three Hands" and make issue #1.

Series premise: In a tropical island city inside the Storyverse, King Kaine Chester once used Lucid Light to control his people. Now redeemed and sworn never to wield that power again, Kaine relies on three elite defenders, the King's Three Hands, to protect his city from Dark Light, a corrupted Anamorphis ruler whose faceless luminous soldiers will stop at nothing to claim Lucid Light and rule the Dream Realm.

Issue premise: Introduce the setting, cover Kaine's reign as the corrupted king, the rise of unrest in the city as rebellion starts to brew, the arrival of the Lucid Lord who calms the people and confronts Kaine, and the cleansing of Kaine's corruption. The cleansing is the main action sequence: Kaine tries to control and fight the Lucid Lord before the Lucid Lord succeeds. The issue ends with the Lucid Lord's warning about the Anamorphis and Dark Light, Kaine's public vow to never again use Lucid Light for control, and Kaine's announcement that he will form a team to help him keep that promise. The Three Hands are not introduced in this issue.
