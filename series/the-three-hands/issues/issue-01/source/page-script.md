# Page Script

All pages are full realistic candid photo-comic pages in 4:5 portrait aspect ratio. Caption-box text must be generated directly inside the page art. Use caption boxes only, no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating subtitles, no blank text boxes, and no later text overlay.

## Page 1: Cover

- Story role: Introduce the series origin and Kaine's moral crisis.
- Layout: Single dramatic cover image.
- Visual beats: King Kaine Chester stands on the royal terrace above the tropical island city, crimson battle suit and purple cheetah-print cape lit by glittering purple Lucid Light. Citizens below appear as small controlled silhouettes. In the clouds above, the Lucid Lord descends with a crystal rainbow sword and golden tether.
- Caption/title text: Cover title text: "The Three Hands". Smaller issue title text: "The Promise of Lucid Light".
- Prompt notes: Realistic superhero photo-comic cover, not painted poster art; readable title text generated inside the cover image.

## Page 2: The City Under Lucid Light

- Story role: Establish the Storyverse island city and Kaine's corrupted rule.
- Layout: Three panels: wide city establishing shot, street-level controlled citizens, Kaine on the terrace.
- Caption boxes: "Inside the Storyverse, an island city glittered with impossible power." "King Kaine Chester called that power protection." "His people felt it as strings."
- Visual beats: Tropical city of white towers, palm-lined avenues, colorful battle-suited citizens moving in synchronized puppet-like steps, purple cosmic energy threads reaching from Kaine's hands over the city.

## Page 3: Rebellion Begins To Breathe

- Story role: Show unrest rising and the city nearing rebellion.
- Layout: Four documentary panels with tense crowd movement.
- Caption boxes: "The city learned to whisper between commands." "Every forced smile became evidence." "By sundown, rebellion had a heartbeat."
- Visual beats: Citizens hide clenched fists, cracked purple control threads, armored workers passing secret signals, crowd gathering below royal steps as Kaine watches from above.

## Page 4: The Lucid Lord Arrives

- Story role: The Lucid Lord calms the people and confronts Kaine.
- Layout: Tall splash panel with two inset crowd panels.
- Caption boxes: "Then Lucid Light answered itself." "The sword did not threaten." "The crowd stopped long enough to breathe."
- Visual beats: The Lucid Lord descends from a Dream Realm rift made of bubble universes, holding the crystal rainbow sword low and open. Golden tether trails like sunlight. Citizens lower their weapons while Kaine's cape snaps in the wind.

## Page 5: Kaine Cannot Command Him

- Story role: Begin the main action sequence as Kaine tries to control and fight the Lucid Lord.
- Layout: Five fast panels.
- Caption boxes: "Kaine reached for the stranger's will." "There was no handle to seize." "Lucid Light could not enslave Lucid Light."
- Visual beats: Purple command ribbons lash toward the Lucid Lord and pass through his cosmic body. Kaine attacks with precise geometric Lucid Light blasts. The Lucid Lord blocks with the rainbow sword, turning impacts into harmless purple sparks.

## Page 6: The Cleansing

- Story role: Main action climax, turning combat into healing.
- Layout: Large central panel surrounded by three smaller action panels.
- Caption boxes: "The king fought like judgment." "The Lucid Lord answered like mercy." "The tether found the wound beneath the crown."
- Visual beats: Kaine levitates in a storm of purple cosmic energy, face twisted by black-purple corruption. The Lucid Lord loops the golden tether around the corruption, touches the rainbow sword to Kaine's chest, and pulls dark shards out as the city watches.

## Page 7: The Warning Of Dark Light

- Story role: Reveal the larger threat and why Kaine must protect Lucid Light.
- Layout: Three panels: exhausted Kaine, Dream Realm vision, Lucid Lord warning.
- Caption boxes: "When the darkness left, shame remained." "Beyond the island, another ruler watched." "Dark Light would come for Lucid Light."
- Visual beats: Kaine kneels on cracked terrace stone, cape torn, eyes clear. The Lucid Lord projects a vision of the Dream Realm boundary: bubble universes, faceless Anamorphis soldiers, and Dark Light shrouded in black corruptive aura.

## Page 8: The Promise

- Story role: Resolve Kaine's redemption and seed the creation of the Three Hands without introducing them.
- Layout: Four panels ending on a wide public vow.
- Caption boxes: "Kaine returned the city to itself." "He swore Lucid Light would never again be a chain." "To keep that promise, he would need hands he could trust."
- Visual beats: Citizens regain free movement as purple threads dissolve. Kaine kneels before the crowd, then stands on the royal terrace with open empty hands. Final wide panel shows the city under sunrise and three empty ceremonial pedestals or banners waiting for future defenders.
