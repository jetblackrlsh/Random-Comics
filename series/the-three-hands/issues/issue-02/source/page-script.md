# Page Script

All pages are full realistic candid photo-comic pages in 4:5 portrait aspect ratio. Caption-box text must be generated directly inside the page art. Use caption boxes only, no speech bubbles, no thought bubbles, no floating subtitles, no blank text boxes, and no later text overlay.

## Page 1: Cover

- Story role: Signal that this is Lucid Lord's backstory and the first open clash with Dark Light.
- Layout: Single dramatic cover image with documentary superhero-photo framing.
- Caption/title text: Cover title text: "The Three Hands". Smaller issue title text: "The Limits of Lucid Light".
- Visual beats: The Lucid Lord stands on a cracked crystalline palace platform, made of glittering purple Lucid Light, crystal rainbow sword in one hand and golden tether in the other. Dark Light looms behind him as a faceless rainbow energy ruler wrapped in pure black aura. Around them, the Dream Realm's bubble universes hover like fragile glass worlds.
- Prompt notes: Readable title text generated inside the cover image. Realistic candid photo-comic cover, not painted poster art.

## Page 2: The Storyverse

- Story role: Explain the Storyverse cosmology and Lucid Light's broad but limited power.
- Layout: Four panels: wide Dream Realm map, close view of a fluid bubble universe bending, rigid bubble universe resisting, Lucid Lord studying both.
- Caption boxes: "The Storyverse was not one world." "Every possible story lived somewhere in the Dream Realm." "Lucid Light could guide the fluid ones." "Written stories did not move."
- Visual beats: Purple nebula Dream Realm filled with bubble universes, some soft and swirling as Lucid Light ribbons shape clouds and roads, others hard like sealed glass with geometric cracks of resistance. Lucid Lord floats between them, faceless and luminous, learning the difference.

## Page 3: Useless At Home

- Story role: Show why Lucid Lord left his rigid homeworld.
- Layout: Three panels: homeworld city, Lucid Lord failing to alter a rigid structure, Anamorphis crowd turning away.
- Caption boxes: "His own world was already written." "There, his greatest gift looked like nothing." "So he left before shame became obedience."
- Visual beats: Rigid Anamorphis Homeworld with symmetrical glass towers and black-rainbow banners. Lucid Lord sends elegant purple Lucid Light arcs toward a broken bridge or frozen civic machine, but the light rebounds harmlessly. Faceless Anamorphis citizens and soldiers stand unimpressed while Dark Light's palace platform waits in the distance.

## Page 4: Proof Across The Bubbles

- Story role: Compress Lucid Lord's adventures and acquisition of the two treasures.
- Layout: Five panels in a travel montage.
- Caption boxes: "He crossed softer stories." "He learned where dreams could still choose." "He claimed a sword that healed." "He found a tether that connected." "Then he went home."
- Visual beats: Lucid Lord traveling through luminous paths between bubble universes, calming a collapsing dream city with purple light, receiving or lifting the crystal rainbow sword of healing from a glowing ruin, catching the golden energy tether from a sunlit cosmic bridge, then turning back toward the rigid homeworld.

## Page 5: Dark Light Sees The Prize

- Story role: Reintroduce Dark Light as ruler and set up the battle.
- Layout: Four panels: Lucid Lord returns, presents his treasures, Dark Light dismisses him, Dark Light focuses on the tether.
- Caption boxes: "Lucid Lord returned with proof." "Dark Light saw no worth in the pilgrim." "He saw worth in the power." "And hunger moved first."
- Visual beats: Palace platform on the rigid homeworld, Anamorphis soldiers in disciplined rows. Lucid Lord holds the rainbow sword low and the golden tether open as an offering. Dark Light stands tall with black aura flickering around rainbow light, ignoring Lucid Lord's plea and staring directly at the golden tether.

## Page 6: The Siphon Charge

- Story role: Main action sequence begins as Dark Light attacks the tether to steal Lucid Lord's Lucid Light.
- Layout: Large diagonal action panel with three smaller impact panels.
- Caption boxes: "Dark Light did not ask." "He charged the connection." "Power answered power." "The tether screamed gold."
- Visual beats: Dark Light launching from the palace platform with impossible speed, black aura trailing like a clean violent halo. He grabs or strikes the golden tether as Lucid Lord braces, purple Lucid Light erupting from his body in geometric arcs. Anamorphis soldiers scatter or lean into the blast wind. The tether bends between them like a live wire.

## Page 7: The Sword Refuses

- Story role: Battle climax and revelation that Dark Light's corruption cannot be healed from inside this rigid story.
- Layout: Six tense panels: Lucid Lord sees the hunger, raises sword, sword repelled, Dark Light's aura remains pure-black, homeworld geometry locks around them, Lucid Lord understands.
- Caption boxes: "The hunger around Dark Light was corruption." "The healing blade knew its purpose." "But the darkness repelled mercy." "This story had already chosen its warlord." "Lucid Light had reached its limit."
- Visual beats: Lucid Lord swings the crystal rainbow sword in a healing gesture toward Dark Light, but the black aura rejects the rainbow blade with a bright impact. Dark Light remains serene and faceless. The homeworld's streets and towers become rigid story-panels around them, implying the entire world refuses change.

## Page 8: Warnings Before War

- Story role: Resolve the issue by widening the conflict to the whole Storyverse.
- Layout: Four panels ending on a broad cosmic threat image.
- Caption boxes: "So Lucid Lord ran." "Not from fear." "From math." "One rigid world had become a war reaching every story."
- Visual beats: Lucid Lord escaping through the Dream Realm Boundary with torn light trailing from his body, warning distant Lucid Light wielders only as silhouettes in separate bubble universes. Behind him, the Anamorphis Storyverse Gateway activates on the rigid homeworld. Final panel shows soldiers stepping through black-rainbow portals toward many bubble universes while Lucid Lord flies ahead, desperate to warn Kaine and others before it is too late.
