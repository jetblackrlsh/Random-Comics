# Character Bible

This issue uses the series continuity files in `series/the-three-hands/source/` and the reference images in `series/the-three-hands/reference-images/`.

## The Lucid Lord

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-lord-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Humanoid Anamorphis made entirely of glittering purple Lucid Light, translucent cosmic body, calm faceless luminous head, no eyes or mouth, futuristic crystal rainbow sword of healing, golden energy tether coiled at one hand. He should look like a living being of purple cosmic light rather than a person in armor.
- Issue state: Starts as an outsider on his own homeworld, grows more confident after adventures, returns hopeful, then leaves wounded but determined after learning Lucid Light cannot alter rigid stories.

## Dark Light

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dark-light-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Tall faceless humanoid made of rainbow luminous energy, smooth alien silhouette, no visible eyes or mouth, crackling black corruptive aura shrouding the body. His posture is still, precise, and predatory; his aura should feel pure in its corruption rather than smoky or messy.
- Issue state: Rules the rigid homeworld from a central palace platform. He initially dismisses Lucid Lord, then attacks the golden tether to siphon Lucid Light.

## Anamorphis Soldiers

- Source entry: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/anamorphis-soldier-character.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Faceless luminous humanoid soldiers, smooth featureless heads, energy-body limbs, flight posture, shapeshifting motion trails, colored rim glow, disciplined army formations.
- Issue state: Serve Dark Light on the homeworld and later deploy through the Storyverse gateway.

## Rigid Anamorphis Homeworld

- Source entry: `../../../source/setting-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/rigid-anamorphis-homeworld-setting.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Monumental crystalline city, pale glass towers, strict symmetry, hard white light, black-rainbow banners, central palace platform, faceless Anamorphis citizens and soldiers moving in orderly formation. The world should feel beautiful but locked, as if nothing can improvise.

## Dream Realm Boundary

- Source entry: `../../../source/setting-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dream-realm-boundary-setting.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Cosmic purple space filled with translucent bubble universes, each bubble reflecting a different story-world, luminous paths between bubbles, nebula mist, floating islands and stars suspended in glassy spheres.

## Lucid Light

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lucid-light-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Glittering purple cosmic energy, star motes, clean geometric ribbons, dreamlike lensing around hands and body, precise arcs that bend flexible dream matter but fail against rigid story structures.

## Crystal Rainbow Sword

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/crystal-rainbow-sword-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Futuristic crystalline sword with rainbow refractions, translucent blade, luminous clean edge. It heals and cleanses, never cuts like a lethal weapon.

## Golden Energy Tether

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/golden-energy-tether-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Flexible golden light cord, braided energy strands, halo-like loops, connection motif, anchored to Lucid Lord's hand. Dark Light grabs at it to siphon Lucid Lord's power.

## Anamorphis Storyverse Gateway

- Source entry: `../../../source/key-item-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/anamorphis-storyverse-gateway-key-item.png`
- Stable page identifiers: Tall ring-shaped portal with white crystalline ribs, black-rainbow energy seams, bubble-universe reflections inside the opening, Anamorphis soldiers stepping through in streaks of light.
