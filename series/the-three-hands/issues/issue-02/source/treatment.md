# Treatment

## Title

The Limits of Lucid Light

## Issue Number

2

## Logline

Before warning Kaine's island city, the Lucid Lord returns to his rigid homeworld to prove his worth, only to discover that even Lucid Light cannot rewrite a story already locked under Dark Light's rule.

## Central Conflict

The Lucid Lord believes that if he gathers enough proof, power, and healing artifacts, his people will finally accept him and his world can be saved. Dark Light believes only power matters, and Lucid Lord's uniqueness is valuable only as something to steal.

## Theme

The highest power still needs humility when the story refuses to bend.

## Ending

The Lucid Lord escapes into the Storyverse instead of dying in a doomed war, choosing to warn every Lucid Light wielder he can find while the Anamorphis launch their own gateway network behind him.

## Emotional Arc

The Lucid Lord moves from lonely shame to adventurous confidence, then horror, grief, and urgent responsibility. Dark Light moves from dismissive ruler to predatory strategist, revealing that his threat is cosmic rather than personal.

## Story Shape

Eight pages: cover, Storyverse cosmology, Lucid Lord's rigid homeworld, his exile and treasure quests, his return to prove himself, Dark Light's siphon attack, the healing sword's failure and Lucid Lord's realization, then his escape and the Anamorphis gateway expansion.
