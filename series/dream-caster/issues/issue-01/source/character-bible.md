# Character Bible

Use these descriptions in every page prompt where the elements appear. This issue belongs to the Dream Caster series, so the series continuity files and reference images are binding.

## Bob Carlson / Dream Caster

- Series source: `series/dream-caster/source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `series/dream-caster/reference-images/bob-carlson-dream-caster-character.png`
- Stable identifiers: tired middle-aged white man in his late 40s or early 50s, average build, pale skin, short thinning brown hair with gray at the temples, tired blue-gray eyes, stubble, soft tired face, practical khaki work jacket over faded station T-shirt, dark jeans, worn sneakers, lanyard ID badge, heavy key ring.
- Storm changes: after the lightning strike, Bob's eyes sometimes glow with TV static, purple Lucid Light reflects across his face and hands, and the Dream Caster remote stays in his right hand unless a transformation changes his body.
- Emotional range: exhausted devotion, startled fear, awe, panic, stubborn heroism, humbled wonder.
