## Lena Ortiz

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/lena-ortiz-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Latina girl around 13, slim build, warm brown skin, dark brown eyes, round face, thick black curly hair tied back with loose curls escaping, yellow rain jacket over blue hoodie, black leggings, worn red sneakers, small sketchbook with map doodles.
- Emotional continuity: Starts tense, perfectionistic, and sleep-deprived. Becomes frightened in the Storyverse. Ends focused, brave, and still realistically uncertain.

## Captain Quill

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/captain-quill-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Amphibious humanoid Dream Guardian with teal-blue skin, gold-flecked eyes, subtle gill marks, short swept-back sea-green hair, lean athletic build, weathered copper-and-purple Lucid Light Conduit armor, ink-stained gloves, and feather-like cartographer spear.
- Emotional continuity: Calm but pressured, protective, tactical, never smug. He mentors Lena by giving her room to make the decisive choice.

## The Eraser

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/the-eraser-nightmare-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Shapeshifting monster made of wet black paper, bone-white eraser dust, ink-smeared claws, long faceless head that can split into false friendly faces, glowing red pencil-scratch eyes, scraps of half-erased maps and failed endings shedding from its body.
- Power continuity: Feeds on fear of mistakes and unfinished work. Senses fear as red trails. Shapeshifts into ruined futures and accusing false faces.

## Mister Sandman

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/mister-sandman-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Tall older Black man with calm eyes, silver-white close-cropped hair, neatly trimmed white beard, long midnight-blue coat over Lucid Light Conduit armor, sand-like golden starlight drifting from his cuffs.
- Usage in this issue: Appears only as a brief Lucid Light signal, establishing the Dream Guardians' larger war without solving Lena's problem.

## Recurring Visual Rules

- Realistic candid photo-comic, documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism feel.
- Caption boxes only, no speech bubbles or thought bubbles.
- Lucid Light is cosmic purple with star-specks and luminous map-line rings.
- Nightmare fear trails are red scratch-like threads or heat trails that the Eraser follows.
- Inkfall Reef should look tactile and physical: coral, tide pools, folded paper-map bridges, ink waterfalls, wet surfaces, and impossible scale.
