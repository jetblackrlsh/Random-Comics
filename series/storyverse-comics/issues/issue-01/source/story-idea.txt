Series Title:
Storyverse Comics

Issue #1 Title:
The Map That Feared the Ending

Premise:
An anxious young mapmaker named Lena Ortiz falls asleep while trying to perfect a school project and wakes in Inkfall Reef, a living map-world floating above the Cosmic Sea. A shapeshifting Nightmare called the Eraser is devouring the reef by feeding on fear of wrong choices and unfinished endings. Lena teams with Captain Quill, a Dream Guardian cartographer, and learns that a map is not proof that the traveler will never get lost. A map is a promise to revise.

Theme:
You do not need a perfect plan before you move. Courage means choosing, learning, and revising when the route changes.

Series Introduction Goals:
- Establish the Storyverse as bubble universes rising from the Cosmic Sea.
- Show that each issue follows a different dreamer in a different world with a different Dream Guardian and Nightmare.
- Show Nightmares as fear-feeding shapeshifters.
- Show Dream Guardians using Lucid Light Conduit armor and strategy.
- Make victory depend on tactics, self-knowledge, and adaptation rather than raw power.
