# Treatment

## Title

Trigger Discipline

## Issue Number

2

## Logline

A reckless laser-tag player dreams into a utopian laser city under drone attack and learns that smart cover, timing, and patience beat charging straight into the fight.

## Central Conflict

Thomas Jerry wants action so badly that he treats every fight like a race to the center. The Overheat Virus turns that habit into mortal danger by taking over a citywide laser system where weapons fail when they are fired without discipline. To survive, Thomas must stop demanding constant offense and use his Lucid Dreamer power to create cover, angles, and time for Sentinel Jax to fight strategically.

## Theme

Power works best when it is controlled.

## Moral Message

Running straight into action and holding the trigger down can feel brave, but discipline wins the match. Cover, patience, and well-timed action protect more people than panic fire.

## Ending

Thomas summons Lucid Light cover instead of another bigger gun. Sentinel Jax uses that cover to defeat the Nightmare-infected drones one precise shot at a time, letting the Lucid Light laser pistol cool between bursts. The laser city recovers, and Thomas wakes in the arena locker room ready to play his next match with patience instead of panic.

## Emotional Arc

Thomas begins embarrassed and defensive after another laser-tag loss, convinced that losing means he simply needs to rush harder and fire more. In the Storyverse, the same habits nearly get him and innocent civilians killed. Seeing a futuristic officer fall because of the exact mistake Thomas keeps making forces him to understand that courage is not constant motion. By the end, Thomas still loves action, but he is ready to use cover, timing, and restraint.

## Story Shape

Eight pages: cover, laser-tag failure, locker-room nap, arrival in Neon Meridian, drone attack and rescue by Sentinel Jax, Thomas creating the Lucid Light laser pistol, the overheated officer's fall, and Thomas choosing cover and timed shots to save the city before waking for the next match.
