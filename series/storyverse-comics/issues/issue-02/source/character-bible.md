# Character Bible

Use the parent series descriptions and reference images in `series/storyverse-comics/source/` and `series/storyverse-comics/reference-images/` as binding continuity for this issue.

## Thomas Jerry

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/thomas-jerry-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Young man around 19, medium brown skin, short tight black curls, expressive dark eyes, lean build, clean-shaven face, red-and-black laser tag vest over a gray hoodie, black athletic pants, scuffed white sneakers, and a plastic arena laser tag blaster with heat-warning lights.
- Emotional continuity: Starts eager, embarrassed, and impulsive. In the Storyverse he becomes frightened by the real cost of his habits, then focused and tactical after choosing cover instead of reckless attack.

## Sentinel Jax

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/sentinel-jax-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Cyborg Dream Guardian in his early 30s with warm tan skin on the human side of his face, cropped black hair, one natural brown eye, one violet cybernetic lens eye, a chrome cybernetic right arm and shoulder, practical matte graphite Dream Guardian Conduit armor with purple Lucid Light seams, compact utility pouches, and calm tactical posture.
- Emotional continuity: Protective, direct, and disciplined. Jax teaches Thomas by making him observe the battlefield before acting.

## The Overheat Virus

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/overheat-virus-drones-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Nightmare computer virus expressed as a swarm of black-and-red laser drones with cracked glass sensor eyes, exposed hot coils, red glitch patterns, smoke from overheated vents, and jagged digital static crawling across their shells.
- Power continuity: Infects laser systems, weapon cooldown controls, city drones, and targeting networks. Feeds on panic, overreaction, and reckless energy expenditure.

## Dream Guardians

- Series source: `../../../source/character-descriptions.md`
- Reference image: `../../../reference-images/dream-guardians-character.png`
- Visible identifiers: Diverse warriors, scouts, healers, and guides in battle-worn Lucid Light Conduit armor. Each Guardian's armor reflects their home world, but all share purple conduit seams, practical protection, and calm readiness under impossible pressure.
- Usage in this issue: Sentinel Jax is a cyborg Dream Guardian whose armor integrates cybernetics and Lucid Light Conduit seams.

## Recurring Visual Rules

- Realistic candid photo-comic, documentary phone-photo or handheld photojournalism feel.
- Caption boxes only, no speech bubbles or thought bubbles.
- Lucid Light is cosmic purple with star-specks, luminous threads, lensing rings, and soft violet reflections.
- The Overheat Virus shows red digital glitches, smoke, hot coils, and weapon-warning colors.
- Neon Meridian should look utopian before the attack: clean public plazas, laser-powered transit, medical beams, garden irrigation arrays, and soft civic lighting.
