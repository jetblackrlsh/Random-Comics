Create Issue 2 of Storyverse Comics.

Premise:

The issue starts with a young man named Thomas Jerry who is struggling to get the hang of playing laser tag.

He always wants to run straight into the action, which leaves him open.

He constantly presses the trigger of his laser tag gun, causing it to overheat and become inoperable.

Disappointed after losing a match, he takes a nap in the arena locker room between matches.

His dream takes him to a futuristic universe in the Storyverse, a utopian city where laser technology has helped to solve all of the world's problems.

But a nightmare computer virus infects the world's laser system and laser drones start attacking the civilians.

Before a drone can strike down Thomas with a laser blast, a cyborg dream guardian tackles Thomas out of the way into an alleyway.

The cyborg tells Thomas to use his lucid dreamer power to make a weapon for him to fight off the drones.

Thomas concentrates and a Lucid Light laser pistol forms in the cyborg's grasp.

The cyborg tells Thomas that the pistol won't be enough.

Thomas is confused and asks the cyborg to just jump out and blast the drones.

The cyborg motions toward a futuristic cop outside the alley who is trying to run at the drones while holding down the trigger of his laser pistol.

Thomas watches as the cop's pistol overheats and the cop is struck down by a laser drone.

Thomas, learning the error of his ways, summons cover out of Lucid Light, which the cyborg uses to duck behind as he takes down the drones one at a time.

The cyborg doesn't just constantly fire his pistol.

He waits between shots to let his pistol cool down.

The drones are defeated and peace is returned to the world.

Thomas wakes up and stands up ready for his next laser tag match.
