# Character And Reference Bible

## Deca-Form

- Role: stranded superhero and survivor.
- Age range: young adult, early 20s.
- Face and skin: freckled tan skin, alert expressive face, dark blue eyes.
- Hair: slicked-back spiky crimson hair, always visible in human form.
- Human outfit: black leather jacket, red cargo jeans, silver metal boots.
- Signature prop: alien super belt with glowing silver body, shimmering neon red light channels, and metallic black segments. The belt is always visible in human form and during transformation moments.
- Body language: starts relaxed and confident, then anxious and shaken, finally focused and quietly content.
- Text notes: "Deca-Form" and story captions are allowed only as generated text inside page art. No later text overlays.

## Butterfly Form

- Winged iridescent humanoid butterfly alien form.
- Humanlike posture, tall and elegant, translucent wings with blue, violet, green, and silver iridescence.
- Face still carries a faint resemblance to Deca-Form through dark blue eyes and confident expression.
- Belt motif persists as a glowing silver, neon red, and black core at the waist.

## Speed Form

- Robotic humanoid jet-powered racecar speedster.
- Sleek metallic limbs, aerodynamic car-shell chest, wheel-like details at shoulders and calves, jet exhaust along the back and ankles.
- Color accents repeat the belt palette: glowing silver, neon red, and metallic black.
- Built for running across water with speed-blur spray and heat shimmer.

## Plant Form

- Humanoid plant alien entirely made of rainbow-colored wooden vines covered in flowers.
- Vines are bark-textured but vivid, with living blossoms in red, violet, yellow, blue, and green.
- Hands release shimmering glowing seeds.
- Can grow bioluminescent vines and fruit gardens from the island soil.

## Amoeba Form

- Humanoid translucent amoeba alien, glossy and semi-fluid, with prismatic internal light.
- The belt motif appears as a glowing waist-core suspended within the transparent body.
- Duplication power appears as the body splitting into several identical humanoid forms.

## Setting

- Cruise ship: modern vacation cruise ship in open ocean, warm deck lights, then storm-darkened sky.
- Island: isolated tropical island with pale sand, dense green palms, volcanic rocks, and no visible ships or civilization.
- New settlement: handmade island village with bioluminescent vine shelters, garden terraces, simple roads, beach paths, and lookout platforms created by different alien transformations.
