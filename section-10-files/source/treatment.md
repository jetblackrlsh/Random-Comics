# The Section 10 Files

## Logline

After UFO disclosure files coincide with a wave of vanished scientists, an Anonymous cell digs through nearly 200 releases and discovers the disappearances point to shapeshifting "Dark Clothed Creatures," forcing the hackers to seek psychic contact with Master Kalen-Li and the hidden Korrendorian guardians of Earth.

## Central Conflict

Anonymous must turn scattered government disclosure files into actionable truth before the Dark Clothed Creatures erase the remaining researchers and compromise public trust from inside the human institutions meant to protect them.

## Theme

Truth is not passive. When the systems that should protect knowledge are infiltrated, ordinary people with courage, patience, and tools can become the immune system of civilization.

## Ending

The hackers complete a risky psychic handshake through a hidden network ritual. The final page shows Master Kalen-Li answering from an underwater Korrendorian base, confirming that Anonymous is no longer digging alone.

## Emotional Arc

The comic opens with paranoia and grief as researchers vanish. It narrows into disciplined investigation, reveals a terrifying nonhuman threat, then pivots into defiant hope as Anonymous reaches out to allies who may have been guiding them all along.

## Cast

- The Narrator: An Anonymous cell lead, seen only as gloved hands, masked lower face, black hoodie, laptop reflections, and first-person captions.
- Anonymous Cell: A small group of brilliant masked hackers in dark hoodies, operating from a cramped room of laptops, monitors, routers, printed files, and evidence boards.
- The Dark Clothed Creatures: Tall, humanoid, malevolent entities in matte black clothing, gloves, and featureless dark coats. Their faces are indistinct or partly wrong, with subtle signs of impersonation and shapeshifting.
- Master Kalen-Li: A calm Korrendorian leader who resembles an elegant human elder with luminous gray eyes, silver hair, smooth copper-brown skin, a simple white-and-teal robe, and an aura of telepathic focus.
- The Korrendorians: Humanlike telepaths from Korrendor with luminous eyes, restrained clothing, advanced scale-shifting technology, and hidden underground and underwater bases.
