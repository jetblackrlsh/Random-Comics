# Character Bible

## Anonymous Narrator and Cell

Anonymous is represented by real humans rather than a mascot: adults in black hoodies, matte white Guy Fawkes-style masks, black gloves, worn keyboards, laptops, routers, cables, printed government files, redaction bars, evidence boards, and screen glow. Faces remain obscured. The narrator is usually shown as black-gloved hands at a keyboard, a white mask reflected in a monitor, or a hooded silhouette leaning toward the screen.

Stable identifiers: black hoodie, matte white mask, black gloves, laptop with terminal windows, crowded hacker workspace, blue-green screen glow, paper files pinned with red string.

## Dark Clothed Creatures

The Dark Clothed Creatures are tall humanoid figures wearing matte black coats, black shirts, black gloves, and dark shoes. They look almost human, but wrong: too-still posture, slightly elongated limbs, blurred facial details, eyes hidden by shadow, or mismatched facial structure when impersonating someone. They should feel like shapeshifting infiltrators without becoming fantasy monsters. They attack through surveillance, impersonation, intimidation, and disappearance.

Stable identifiers: full dark clothing, shadowed face, unnaturally still posture, subtle body distortion, institutional corridors or streetlight shadows.

## Master Kalen-Li

Master Kalen-Li is the Korrendorian leader: an elegant humanlike elder with copper-brown skin, silver-white hair pulled back, luminous gray eyes, calm lined face, and a simple white-and-teal robe with subtle geometric trim. He carries no weapon. His power is conveyed through stillness, soft light, telepathic ripple effects, and advanced technology around him.

Stable identifiers: silver-white hair, luminous gray eyes, white-and-teal robe, calm expression, underwater or underground Korrendorian base, translucent holographic interfaces.

## Korrendorians

Korrendorians resemble humans but have a quiet, luminous presence. They have gray or violet luminous eyes, simple white/teal/graphite clothing, compact devices at the wrist or collar, and scale-shifting technology shown through miniature figures beside ordinary objects or giant silhouettes in distant water. They are guardians, not conquerors.

Stable identifiers: luminous eyes, humanlike faces, clean advanced technology, hidden bases underground and underwater, size-change visual motifs.
